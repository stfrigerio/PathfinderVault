> **Bruthazmus** is a [[Bugbear|bugbear]] [[Mechanics/Rules/traits/ranger|ranger]] known to raid the [[Lost Coast Road]] from the northeastern [[Nettlewood]]. He particularly hates [[Elf|elves]] and wears a necklace of elf ears.


## Associates

> Bruthazmus is known to work as a bodyguard for [[Nualia Tobyn]] and frequently gets in fights with [[Shalelu Andosana]] of [[Sandpoint]].
