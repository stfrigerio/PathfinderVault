> **Haliduras Karn** is (or was; his current status is unknown) a member of the [[Aspis Consortium]]. The Aspis tasked him to explore the [[Jistka Imperium|Jistkan]] ruins of [[Rachikan]] on [[Cheliax|Cheliaxs]] southwestern coast, which had recently been discovered by the [[Pathfinder]] [[Eldis Grone]].


## Relatives

> Haliduras's half-brother [[Zahur Karn]] led guards for the expedition, and his daughter [[Marianix Karn]] would later lead an expedition to Rachikan on behalf of the [[Grand Athenaeum]].
