> **Baroness Urgalaena** is the bloodthirsty, enthusiastic [[Jadwiga]] ruler of the town of [[Saarbotten]], a border town on the [[Thundering River]] in the [[Irrisen|Irriseni]] province known as the [[Verge]]. Urgalaena hosts the vicious "Crimson Tournaments" in town every week. She publicly mocks her sister [[Nadya|Baroness Nadya]] of [[Dalun]] for her diplomatic efforts in order to improve trade with the [[Ulfen]] people.
