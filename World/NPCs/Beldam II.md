> **Grand Prince Beldam II** was [[Grand Prince]] of [[Taldor]] during the 45th century [[Absalom Reckoning|AR]], dying in [[4499 AR]] from [[Disease|illness]]. He had no children of his own, and adopted a powerful Taldan nobleman named [[Micheaux]] in [[4498 AR]] to succeed him.
> One of the his acts during his time as Emperor of Taldor was to strike the Adella family from the nobility, erasing the family entirely from Taldan history books.
