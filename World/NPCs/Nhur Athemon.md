> **Nhur Athemon** is a long-dead exiled noble of the [[Azlant|Azlanti empire]] who was responsible for founding an [[Azlanti (human ethnicity)|Azlanti]] settlement in the [[Echo Wood]] over 10,000 years ago. He was a [[Wizard|wizard-lord]] and a devotee of the [[Demon lord|demon lord]] [[Abraxas]]. After building a sizable stronghold, he was eventually hunted down and slain by Azlanti forces. Despite this, a complex of tunnels underneath his stronghold were overlooked by his hunters and have survived to present day under the town of [[Thornkeep]].
> Nhur Athemon had three powerful apprentices: Vuzhon the [[Enchanter|enchantress]], Daegros the [[Conjurer|conjurer]], and Togarin the [[Necromancer|necromancer]].
