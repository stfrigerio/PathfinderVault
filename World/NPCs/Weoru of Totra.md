> **Weoru of Totra** serves as the biologist archivist aboard the *[[Grinning Pixie]]*, the [[Pathfinder Society]]'s [[Arcadian Mariner's Lodge|Arcadian Mariners Lodge]]. He has worked with the *Pixie*'s current captain, [[Eras the Needle]], for more than a decade. Weoru is responsible for organizing the *Pixie*'s maps, holds, and findings, including its study of marine wildlife.


## Background

> Weoru was trained in [[Sothis]], [[Osirion]], as an embalmer and specializes in preserving specimens of rare creatures. He is also a capable sailor.
