> **Lord Slissk** is an iridescent-scaled [[Naga|naga]] and one of the three monster leaders of a band of aquatic [[Slavery|slavers]] who control [[Scrawny Crossing]]. The three monsters serve a telepathic overlord believed to be an [[Alghollthu master|alghollthu master]]. The band is charged with gathering fresh [[Humanoid|humanoid]] slaves and bringing them to overlord's hidden lair for experimentation and cross-breeding purposes.
