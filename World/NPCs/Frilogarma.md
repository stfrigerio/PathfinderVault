> **Queen Frilogarma** is the sadistic [[Bestiary/Nymph|nymph]] [[Sorcerer|sorcerer]] who rules the [[Court of Ether]] in the [[Nar-Voth]] region of the [[Darklands]]. She and her [[Dark fey|dark fey]] followers were exiled to [[Golarion]] from the [[First World]] during a previous age, and are kept under careful surveillance by the [[Svirfneblin|svirfneblin]] and their elemental allies.


## Relations with other Races

> Queen Frilogarma's government has a truce with the [[Dark folk|dark folk]] who inhabit the [[Shadow Caverns]], one of the closest major entrance to the caverns of Nar-Voth from the surface. She uses them as a first line of defense against any incursion from above.
