> **Truan Iolavai** was an ancient explorer who made a legendary journey across [[Avistan]] and [[Casmaron]] that was chronicled in the *[[Record of Truan Iolavai]]*.


## Iobaria

> Near the beginning of their journey, Truan and his companions discovered the ancient edifice of [[Hask-Ultharan]] in the pine forests of [[Iobaria]]. A gigantic stone cairn, it was marked with the weathered script of the [[Cyclops|cyclopes]], who ruled the land during the [[Age of Legend]], and whose stones served travelers in that land as a way marker. When their guides refused to approach it, Truan investigated, but was stopped by a howling mob of malformed [[Giant|giants]]. These creatures were said to have been led by the [[Demon lord|demon lord]] [[Kostchtchie]] himself, who crushed more than a dozen strong warriors before the [[Human|humans]] fled for safety.
> Even though elements of this story has been critiqued by scholars throughout the centuries, its basic facts remain uncontested.


## Kaladay

> Iolavai is thought to have eventually reached the far side of Casmaron, and explored the distant [[Kaladay]] province of [[Yen-Shuan]].
