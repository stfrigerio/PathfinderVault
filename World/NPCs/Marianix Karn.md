> **Marianix Karn** is an [[Archaeology|archaeologist]] for the [[Grand Athenaeum]] leading an expedition in the lost [[Jistka Imperium]] settlement of [[Rachikan]], but is also secretly a spy for the [[Pathfinder Society]] recruited by [[Zarta Dralneen]].



## Relatives

> Marianix is the daughter of [[Haliduras Karn]], a high-ranking member of the [[Aspis Consortium]] last known to have been at the Rachikan expedition site.


## Associates

> Marianix was a lover of Paralictor Maladin Bane, a [[Hellknights|Hellknight]] assigned to protect the Rachikan expedition.


## Background

> Zarta Dralneen, a [[Cheliax faction|Chelish]] member of the Pathfinder Society, recruited Marianix to the Pathfinder Society as part of a mission the Society dispatched to infiltrate the expedition in [[4710 AR]].
