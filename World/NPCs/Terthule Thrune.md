> **Terthule** was the third member of [[House Thrune]] to ascend the throne of [[Cheliax]] following the [[Chelish Civil War]]. He inherited the kingdom in [[4674 AR]] after his cousin [[Antoninus Thrune|Antoninus]] ate a safu'u fish dinner that was not well-prepared. Terthule was succeed by his cousin, [[Carellia Thrune|Carellia]], in [[4682 AR]] after he mysteriously disappeared.
