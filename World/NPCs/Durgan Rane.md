> **Durgan Rane** was the chairman of the [[Cailean Council]], one of the [[Revolutionary Council|Revolutionary Councils]] that ruled [[Galt]] for a short period of time. Under his leadership, Galt changed the alphabet for [[Common]], along with the names for the days of the week. It also instituted martial law and mandatory conscription. Rane was killed in a *putsch* by his own, newly disciplined soldiers soon thereafter.
