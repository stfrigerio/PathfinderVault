> The **Priest-King Kortash Khain** it the autocratic ruler of the hidden [[Ghoul|ghoul]] city of [[Nemret Noktoria]] in [[Sekamina]], which he founded. He is also [[Golarion|Golarions]] High Priest of the [[Demon lord|demon lord]] [[Kabriri]], patron to all ghouls, and is one of the most powerful [[Spellcaster|spellcasters]] on the planet. He rarely leaves the city, which he someday hopes to turn into an unliving version of Kabriri's [[Abyssal realm]] of [[Everglut]].


## Foreign relations

> Khain maintains open diplomatic ties and conducts trade agreements with the nations of [[Thuvia]] and [[Osirion]] on the surface of [[Garund]], but is also said to have made secret agreements with other nations as well. He is also known to wage war on his [[Darklands]] neighbors and Osirion when it serves his city's interests.


## Advisors

> The Priest-King is said to be served by group of powerful [[Ghul|great ghul]] advisors, who are also priests of Kabriri. They are said to have the heads of animals and know secret passages to the surface.
