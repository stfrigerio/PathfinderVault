> **Sonnorae Imlios** was the last of a powerful [[Varisian]] family that carefully preserved lore about the Varisian way of life during the [[Age of Darkness]]. To preserve her family's years of work, Sonnorae created a [[Demiplane|demiplane]] called the [[Harrowed Realm]], which she created from the [[Dimension of Dreams]] and linked her new plane to an ancient [[Harrow deck|harrow deck]], known as the *[[Deck of Harrowed Tales]]*. Sonnorae's creation eventually turned against her and she was murdered by eight of her [[Storykin|storykin]] known as the [[Storykin|Conspirators]].
