> **Eriah** was a strong adventurer killed by the wizard [[Arcil]] following a drunken row with him about [[Elyana Sadrastis]] on the eve of the marriage of [[Stelan]] to [[Lenelle]]. He was the twin brother of fellow adventurer [[Daramont]] with whom he shared good looks and sandy hair; he was present when his brother fell in battle with an [[Umbral dragon|umbral dragon]]. In addition to his twin, Eriah adventured with [[Elyana Sadrastis]], [[Arcil]], [[Stelan]] and [[Vallyn]]
