> **Nadara** was the niece of the infamous and evil [[Shadow wizard|shadow wizard]] called [[Lathroft]]. She was present at her uncle's final defeat at the hands of [[Elyana Sadrastis]], after aiding Elyana's party find her uncle's lair. Like her uncle, she was also a wizard and made her home in [[Woodsedge]] in [[Galt]]. In appearance, Nadara was slim and considered by many to be pretty. She had a particular friendship with the[[Bard's End|bard]]], [[Vallyn]].
> At some point, especially difficult to judge, Nadara turned to evil and gained power within the [[Gray Gardeners]] organization, helped by the knowledge gained from her uncle's notes. She planned to use the [[Crown of Twilight]] to unite the various Galtan factions and gain great power in that way. Her plans were eventually thwarted by Elyana Sadrastis and her companions. Nadara, herself, was killed by Elyana in a final battle for possession of the Crown of Twilight in [[4711 AR]] at Woodsedge.
