> **The Dark Rider** has existed in the [[Dark Forest]] beneath [[Kaer Maga]] for ages—[[Runelords|Runelord]] [[Karzoug]] personally discovered him there. The Dark Rider is a particularly powerful [[Dullahan|dullahan]]; he is able to behead his victims with ease, keep these heads for use as [[Negative energy|negative energy]] missiles, and turn his victims into [[Shadow mastiff|shadow mastiffs]] to serve him.
> Approximately once each year, the Dark Rider tracks down a new victim somewhere in northwest [[Avistan]].
