> **Nyrinda Shraen** is one of the few [[Drow|drow]] of [[House Shraen]] who survived the ancient exodus out of [[Zirnakaynin]] and into the [[Orv|Orvian]] vault of the [[Black Desert]]. As with all survivors of House Shraen, Nyrinda is [[Undead|undead]], an ancient and powerful [[Vampire|vampire]]. Currently one of the ruling members of House Shraen, Nyrinda resides in the central pyramid of [[Shraen]] and is in charge of destroying or driving out the [[Urdefhan|urdefhan]] who have come to inhabit the Black Desert.
