> The [[Intellect devourer|intellect devourer]] [[Sorcerer|sorcerer]] **Tiluatchek** can usually be found in his [[Laboratory|laboratory]], in the [[High IlvarandinGrand Spire|Grand Spire]] of [[High Ilvarandin]] in the [[Darklands]] region of [[Orv]].
> His current host body is the physically powerful [[Ulfen]] warrior Sigmar, but he has numerous others stored away, ranging from [[Storm giant|storm giants]] to a [[Succubus|succubus]], and even an [[Umbral dragon|umbral dragon]]. Included among these bodies is the dead poet [[Vumeshki]] and Kaleb Valdemar, a resident of [[Magnimar]].
> From his location in the Grand Spire, Tiluatchek watches over the [[Dream lens|dream lens]] and oversees the manufacture of the drug [[Midnight milk|midnight milk]]. The [[Midnight Dawn]] conspiracy serves the latter ends, which reach the surface in [[Magnimar]].
