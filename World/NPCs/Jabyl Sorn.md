> **Jabyl Sorn** is the caretaker of the [[House of Blue Stones]] in [[Sandpoint]]. A [[Monk|monk]] devoted to the faith of [[Irori]], Jabyl has tended to the monastery since the death of her father, [[Enderaki Sorn]], in [[4700 AR]]. In her efforts to perfect herself, Jabyl frequently studies in her vast library.
