> **Pasha Muhlia Al-Jakri** (sometimes spelled **Muhlia al'Jakri**) is a former leader of the [[Qadira faction|Qadiran faction]] of the [[Pathfinder Society]]. She resigned from the Society after killing her main rival in the organization, [[Jacquo Dalsine|Baron Jacquo Dalsine]] of the [[Taldor faction]] after his cousin, [[Chalfon Dalsine]], was implicated in her sister's death.
