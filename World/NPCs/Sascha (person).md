> **Sascha** is a daughter of [[Baba Yaga]]. She was the seventh Queen of [[Irrisen]]. She reigned from [[3913 AR]] until [[4013 AR]], when she was removed from [[Golarion]] and her sister [[Karina]] was placed on the throne.
> The family of the previous queen, [[Harcatha]], felt they were being wrongly treated by the new Queen and her family, and in [[3937 AR]] they rose up in what came to be known as the [[Harcatha Rebellion]].
