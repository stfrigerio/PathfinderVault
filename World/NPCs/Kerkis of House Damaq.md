> **Lord Kerkis of House Damaq** is Scion Lord of [[House Damaq]] and Lord Exchequer of the [[Absalom Mint]].



## Appearance

> Lord Kerkis is a [[Keleshite human ethnicity|Keleshite]] [[Human|human]] man in his fifties. He has a slim build, trimmed beard, and bushy eyebrows.


## Personality

> A proud but socially reserved[[Aristocrat|aristocrat]]], Lord Kerkis does not suffer boorish behavior.


## Politics

> Lord Kerkis maintains loyalty to [[Qadira]] and finds bigotry against Keleshites distasteful.
