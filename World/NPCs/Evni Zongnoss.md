> **Evni Zongnoss** helped evacuate civilians from the city of [[Vigil]] during its destruction at the hands of the [[Tar-Baphon|Whispering Tyrant]]. She was the [[Pathfinder Society]] [[Venture-captain|venture-captain]] of the [[Pathfinder lodge|lodge]] in Vigil, but has taken on management of the [[Vodavani Lodge]] in [[Caliphas city|Caliphas]] in place of the overextended healer [[Beaurigmand Trice|Dr Beaurigmand Trice]]. Despite the loss of her lodge, she remains resolute in carrying out her duties.



## Appearance

> Evni is a green-eyed, brown-haired, light-skinned [[Gnome|gnome]] easily identifiable by her large turban and [[Desna|Desnan]] robes.


## Background

> The gnome is a renowned fortune-teller. Evni is noted for her attentiveness with her agents and tends to mother them just a bit.
> When Evni ran the Pathfinder lodge in Vigil, it was renowned as a source of [[Magical item|magical items]], secrets, and lore.
