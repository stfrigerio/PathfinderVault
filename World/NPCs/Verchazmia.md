> **Verchazmia** was an infamous and cruel [[Green dragon|green dragon]] resident in the [[Mierani Forest]] in [[Varisia]]. She was a thorn in the side of the [[Elf|elves]] of the forest and Verchazmia met her end battling the elves at [[Crying Leaf]]. This death is her second and since then nothing has been heard from her. Her cruel ways and ambitions against the elves are now taken on by her son [[Razorhorn]].
