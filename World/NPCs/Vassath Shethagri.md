> **First Mother Vassath Shethagri** is the [[Naga|naga]] ruler of the nation of [[Nagajor]]. Vassath is a [[Royal naga|royal naga]] descended from a long line of naga queens who have ruled Nagajor for millennia, claiming their heritage originates from the [[Deity|goddess]] [[Nalinivati]] who once ruled the nation. Vassath rules from the city of [[Zom Kullan]] and it is claimed that it is only through her [[Ritual magic|rituals]] and those of the other royal family members that the [[Volcano|volcano]] that Zom Kullan is built around is kept from erupting.
