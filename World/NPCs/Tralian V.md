> **Grand Prince Tralian V** was a [[Grand Prince]] of [[Taldor]], and second son of the previous Grand Prince, Penticus IV. He ascended to the throne when his older brother Penticus V committed suicide with a relic known as the *[[Scion's Dagger|Scions Dagger]]*.
> The span of his reign is unknown, although he inaugurated the [[Fourth Army of Exploration]] in the 17th century [[Absalom Reckoning|AR]]. [[Galitian Maramaxus]], a hero of the Fourth Army, was reportedly buried with a brooch awarded by Tralian V.
