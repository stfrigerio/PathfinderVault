> **Azrikalis** is a member of the [[Marrow Reavers]] adventuring group. He was once, some two hundred years ago, the [[Familiar|familiar]] of the archmage Khasekhem from [[Osirion]], until Khasekhem was executed stranding Azrikalis on [[Golarion]]. Currently, and unknown to the [[Quasit|quasit]], Azrikalis is controlled by the powerful [[Lamashtu]] follower, [[Alashra]], the mother of [[Najak]].
