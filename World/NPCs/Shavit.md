> **Rajah Shavit** is the ruler of the city of [[Ukhrul]] in the [[Wide Water]] region of [[Vudra]]. Rajah Shavit is renowned for his expansionist tendencies, he is forever greedily eyeing the neighbouring kingdoms surrounding his. Ukhrul is also known for the [[Firearm|firearms]] it produces, but Rajah Shavit is careful never to sell any of these weapons to nearby kingdoms that could be potential targets for his future conquests.
