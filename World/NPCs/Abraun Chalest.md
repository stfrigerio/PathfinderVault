> **Abraun Chalest** serves as the a curator at the [[Quarterfaux Archives]] but is also secretly a member of the [[Esoteric Order of the Palatine Eye]].


## Appearance

> Abraun is an older, rounder gentleman. He wears a pair of ornate [[Silver|silver]] spectacles and normally has a pleasant smile to accompany his loud laugh.


## Personality

> Abraun is very personable and easy to like, he does not put on any aristocratic airs, unlike many of the other members of Esoteric Order. Abraun works as a curator at the Quarterfaux Archives in [[Caliphas city|Caliphas]] while also secretly serving as the caretaker of the Esoteric Vaults beneath the Haraday Theater and Abraun will sometimes 'borrow' items from the Quarterfaux Archives that are useful to the Esoteric Order. Abraun is also a renowned [[Osirionology|Osirionologist]] and is well travelled as a result of his studies.
