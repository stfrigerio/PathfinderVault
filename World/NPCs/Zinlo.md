> **Prince Zinlo** is the ruler of [[Aspenthar]], one of the five allied city-states of [[Thuvia]]. He is an ambitious man, and all of his subjects are required to undergo military training. He has also been cultivating links with the [[Water Lord|Water Lords]] of Thuvia's interior. Some suspect he has plans to hijack shipments of the *[[Sun orchid elixir|sun orchid elixir]]* bound for other cities, or even to kidnap the elixir's creator, the [[Alchemist|alchemist]] [[Artokus Kirran]].
