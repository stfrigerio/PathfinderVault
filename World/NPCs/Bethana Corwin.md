> **Bethana Corwin** is a timid, elderly [[Halfling|halfling]] woman who works at [[Rusty Dragon|The Rusty Dragon]] inn in the [[Varisia|Varisian]] town of [[Sandpoint]], handling the maid services and room rentals. She is a very close friend of the inn's owner, [[Ameiko Kaijitsu]]. Bethana is as introverted and quiet as Ameiko is extroverted and chatty. A long-time employee of the Rusty Dragon, Ameiko has been teaching her to read [[Minkaian]] for a number of years, and Bethana as achieved some basic proficiency in the language.
