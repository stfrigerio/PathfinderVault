> **Anneshalli Suwaat** is the chief priest of the  [[Temple of Dahak Dragonsthrall|temple of Dahak]] in the town of [[Dragonsthrall]] on [[Dahak's Horn|Dahaks Horn]] island in the [[Shackles]]. He is a squat, beady-eyed bully of an [[Osirion|Osirian]] who often abuses his authority over the town's inhabitants. He answers only to [[Houkna Bewul]], brutal master of the town and captain of the [[Ship|frigate]] *[[Black Dragon ship|Black Dragon]]*.
