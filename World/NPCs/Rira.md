> **Rira** is a notorious [[Pirate|pirate]] captain who now serves under the [[Pathfinder Society|Pathfinder]] captain [[Challys Argent]] as one of her so-called [[Devil's Four|Devils Four]].


## Appearance

> From the neck down, Rira dresses and appears like a pirate queen, wearing a decadent yet functional dress with a plunging neck line, her tanned skin dotted with pirate tattoos. However, Rira wears a terrifying mask fashioned in the image of a ferocious [[Sahuagin|sahuagin]], made of a dull grey [[Metal|metal]] that is covered in green patina and boasts a huge mane of bleached hair. In battle, Rira wields a [[Magic|magical]] cutlass, however she more often uses [[Spell|spells]] as Rira is an accomplished [[Arcane|arcanist]].
