> **Pevrin Elkarid** is the oldest son of [[Zokar Elkarid]] and lives in the town of [[Ravengro]] in the [[Canterwall]] county of the nation of [[Ustalav]]. He has been the town's post boy for three years now, which entails making copies of any bulletins and posting them to the five [[Posting Poles]] around town; he charges 1 [[Copper piece|copper piece]] for this service.
