> **Baron Chalfon Dalsine** was a member of both the [[Pathfinder Society]] and later the [[Shadow Lodge faction]] that fought against the [[Decemvirate]]. He was a cousin of [[Jacquo Dalsine|Baron Jacquo Dalsine]], leader of the Pathfinder Society's [[Taldor faction]], and engineered the death of his cousin by [[Muhlia Al-Jakri|Pasha Muhlia Al-Jakri]]. He was killed in his home in [[Oppara]] by [[Pathfinder|Pathfinders]] in [[4711 AR]] after they uncovered his plot.
