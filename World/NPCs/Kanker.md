> **Kanker** is a [[Ghast|ghast]] priest of [[Kabriri]] who lives in underground warrens that connect tunnels under [[Paupers' Graves|Paupers Graves]], [[Devil's Platter|Devils Platter]], and [[Sandpoint]], among other areas of the greater-Sandpoint region. In addition to being a priest, Kanker has some [[Alchemical|alchemical]] skill, having developed a method for transforming [[PathfinderWiki/Creatures/Dog|dogs]] into [[Ghoul hound|ghoul hounds]]. He is also working for [[Tiluatchek]] on a version of [[Midnight milk|midnight milk]] that can be put into a town's water supply.
> Kanker is responsible for turning the [[Rogue|rogue]] [[Jediah Kheln]] into a [[Ghoul|ghoul]] after tricking him into removing the protective medallion of [[Ashava]] from Paupers' Grave.
