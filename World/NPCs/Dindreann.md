> **Dindreann** is the high priestess of [[Calistria's House of Stolen Kisses|Calistrias House of Stolen Kisses]], both a temple of [[Calistria]] and a brothel in the center of [[Quent]], the capital of [[Motaku Isle]] in the [[Shackles]]. The temple is one of the best information brokerages in the Shackles as many people share their secrets with the temple's sacred prostitutes in the heat of passion. Dindreann is a friend of Quent's ruler, [[Tessa Fairwind]].
