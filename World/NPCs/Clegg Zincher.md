> The owner of [[Zincher's Arena|Zinchers Arena]] in [[Riddleport]] and numerous other fighting venues around the region, **Clegg Zincher** runs all the gambling on these fighting matches. Zincher also runs most of the organized manual labor in Riddleport, which gives him the power to organize strikes and industrial sabotage.
> At one point, Clegg had a brother who worked as an [[Alchemist|alchemist]]. This brother was killed by [[Orik Vancaskerkin]] after he sold the latter a counterfeit *elixir of love*, forcing Orik to flee Riddleport to escape Clegg's retribution.
> His [[Zincher's Tenement|home]] is located in the [[Leeward District]].
