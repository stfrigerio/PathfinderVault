> **Lady Sutarai-Gongen** is the current leader of the divided [[World/Culture/Races/Tengu|tengu]] nation of [[Kwanlai]]. She is a highly powerful [[Half-celestial|half-celestial]] [[Cleric|cleric]] of [[Hei Feng]] and rules partly as a spiritual leader and partly as a monarch. She has the allegiance of all five major tengu clans. She resides in the capital city of [[Hisuikarasu]].
