> **Sarevi Tilernos** is a young [[Human|human]] woman and fourth or fifth child of [[House Tilernos]], a [[Noble houses of Cheliax|Chelish noble house]] with ties to [[Cheliax]] and [[World/Geography/Nations/Lastwall]]. In [[4719 AR]], she joined with several childhood friends from rival houses — [[Jasper Caperina]], [[Amyas Charthagnion]], [[Leonie Ciucci]], and [[Dacian Julistarc]] — on an expedition to the [[Menador Mountains]]. She prominently wears her family crest on a brooch, and had a sister who was a [[Knights of Ozem|Knight of Ozem]] killed during the destruction of [[World/Geography/Nations/Lastwall]].
