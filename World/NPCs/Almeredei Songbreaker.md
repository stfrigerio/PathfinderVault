> **Almeredei Songbreaker** leads the Scions of Deceit within the [[Imvrildarai]], an ancient order of militaristic [[Calistria|Calistrian]] priests in the southern [[Fierani Forest]] of [[Kyonin]].



## Associates

> Songbreaker was friends with the sect's former High Priest, [[Rekaereil Sandurei]], who was assassinated by [[Cambion|cambions]] in [[4714 AR]].


## Background

> Songbreaker is an expert in cryptography and stealth who views the Imvrildarai as guardians of dangerous lore instead of a sect of soldiers.


## Possessions

> She keeps a *mirror of opposition* under lock and key, as well as the [[Nabasu|nabasu]] Anochnodan held captive in an *iron flask*.
