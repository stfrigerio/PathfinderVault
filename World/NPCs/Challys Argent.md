> **Challys Argent** is the [[Pirate|pirate]] captain of the [[Ship|galleon]] *[[Aspidochelone ship|Aspidochelone]]* and a [[Pathfinder Society]] [[Venture-captain|venture-captain]].



## Background

> Challys was once a scholar following her family tradition, studying at a seaside eyrie. [[Pirate|Pirates]] raided it and killed her fellow pedants, leading her to swear vengeance on their behalf and seek the [[Artifact|artifacts]] stolen from them.


## Personality

> Despite her role as an [[Adventurer|adventurer]], Challys Argent is not quick to anger and is often willing to parlay with fearsome creatures like [[Cyclops|cyclopes]] that others would sooner cut down.


## Companions

> Through the [[Magic item|magic]] cutlass *Siren Call*, she compels four unwilling pirates to her service:

> - the [[Cannibal|cannibal]] [[Ogre|ogre]] [[Otondo]]
> - the noble [[Adalbert Aspodell]]
> - [[Seagrave]]
> - deposed pirate queen [[Rira]], her face permanently hidden by a [[Metal|metal]] mask
