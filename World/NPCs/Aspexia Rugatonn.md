> **Most High Aspexia Rugatonn** holds the position of Grand High Priestess of [[Asmodeus]] in the nation of [[Cheliax]]. As the leader of the official state religion, Aspexia Rugatonn is rightfully respected and feared by her peers, and only members of the ruling [[House Thrune]] would dare to challenge her directly.
> Her son, [[Ludoviro Scarpo]], who is also a [[Cleric|cleric]] of Asmodeus, took his father's name in order to not been seen as riding his mother's coattails.
