> **Saria Roccin** is a [[Durotas|durotas]] (city guard captain) of the [[Westcrown Dottari]]. She is in command of he largest corps of troops of any of the civil leaders in [[Westcrown]], the common dottari whose responsibilities are to patrol the city streets and man the gates of [[City districts of WestcrownParego Spera|Spera]]. Saria keeps her contempt of the [[Hellknight (Book)|Hellknights]] secret, and only begrudging works in co-ordination with them.
