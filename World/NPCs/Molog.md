> **Chief Molog** is the leader of the [[Orc|orc]] [[Wingripper tribe]] who train and ride the [[Wyvern|wyverns]] in and around the city of [[Wyvernsting]] in the northern [[Hold of Belkzen]]. Molog rides an ancient wyvern that is twice as large as usual and boasts an exceptionally deadly [[Poison|poison]]. Members of the Wingripper tribe are renowned for their dislike of touching the ground and thrill-seeking recklessness.
