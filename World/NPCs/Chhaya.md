> **Chhaya** was a princess from the jungles of [[Vudra]]. In the middle 500s [[Absalom Reckoning|AR]], she united the bulk of the [[Mahajanapada|mahajanapadas]]. [[Khiben-Sald]] assassinated her in her palace, and completed the unification. Her [[Shadow-palace of Chhaya|haunted palace]] still stands and is a major destination for explorers and [[Adventurer|adventurers]], given the great wealth in jewels she amassed during her lifetime.
