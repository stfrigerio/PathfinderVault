> **Little Shaggard** is the son of [[Shaggard Halleck]], one of the two founders of the town of [[Lilywhite]] on [[Motaku Isle]] in the [[Shackles]]. Since the death of his father, in [[4692 AR]], Little Shaggard and [[Delemona Burie]] have acted as joint [[Pirate lord|pirate lords]] of Lilywhite. Little Shaggard has taken on the captaincy of his father's [[Ship|barkentine]], *[[Mollusk]]*.
