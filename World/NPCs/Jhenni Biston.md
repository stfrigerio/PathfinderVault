> **Jhenni Biston** was a [[Korvosa|Korvosan]] [[Adventurer|adventurer]] who founded the [[Biston|town that now carries her name]] in [[4574 AR]], when she and her companions cleared the cliffside caves on the banks of [[Lake Syrantula]] of [[Troglodyte|troglodytes]]. Her grandson, Randas Biston, led a separatist group who tried to break free of Korvosan control but were brutally crushed by the [[Sable Company]] and the [[Korvosan Guard]].
