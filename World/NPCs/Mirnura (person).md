> **Mirnura** was the wife of the legendary hero [[Arustun]], the founder of the [[Jistka Imperium]]: the first [[Human|human]] civilization to emerge since the [[Golarion|Golarion-wide]] destruction caused by [[Earthfall]]. Venistos, a friend of Arustun and Mirnura helped the hero crafting a [[Ship|ship]] to sail the [[Inner Sea]] from shore to shore in his quest for civilization. Arustun returned home from his odyssey in [[-4120 AR]] with many foreign associates and allies. He founded the Jistka Imperium in northwestern [[Garund]] and named [[Mirnura|the capital]] after his beloved wife who had remained behind for all those years, helping the city.
