**Athalos** was a very powerful [[Shadow wizard|shadow wizard]]: an expert in [[Shadow magic|shadow magic]]. In Athalos' keep was a map, hidden in an ugly statuette, that showed the whereabouts of the [[Crown of Twilight]], an item that Athalos sought to increase his power. The statuette was removed from his keep by [[Elyana Sadrastis]]' party as loot and retained by [[Stelan|Baron Stelan]] in [[Adrast]], [[Taldor]].
