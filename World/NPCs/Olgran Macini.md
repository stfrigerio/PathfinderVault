> **Olgran Macini** was one of the key figures in the early history of the colony of [[Sargava]], rising to the rank of Grand Praetor.
> He established the cattle ranch of [[Freehold]] as a family holiday estate, and started the ranch's tradition of treating the native [[Mwangi human ethnicity|Mwangi]] as equals. This tradition continues today under his great-great-granddaughter [[Mindra]].
