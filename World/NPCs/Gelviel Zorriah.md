> **Captain Gelviel Zorriah** was an [[Elf|elven]] [[Astronomy|astronomer]] from [[Lirgen]] whose visions from a [[Magic|magical]] [[Orrery of distant worlds|orrery]] inspired the construction of the *[[Lirgen's Glory|Lirgens Glory]]*, an ill-fated [[Spaceship|spaceship]] launched from [[Oagon]] in [[4600 AR]].
> Gelviel's visions culminated in the plans for the *[[Lirgen's Glory|Lirgens Glory]]* in [[4598 AR]]. Believing he was inspired by [[Desna]], Gelviel recruited a crew, built the ship, and launched it into space.
> During their expedition, Gelviel and the *[[Lirgen's Glory|Lirgens Glory]]* encountered the true source of his visions: the [[Emissary from Beyond]]. The Emissary warped the [[Dream crystal|dream crystals]] Gelviel's crew used to record their discoveries to twist all of them into bestial [[Id mutant|id mutants]]. Gelviel took the form of a [[PathfinderWiki/Creatures/Frog|frog]].
