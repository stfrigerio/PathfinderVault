> **Dancing Darla Madile** is one of the three [[Pirate|pirate]] captains that run [[Firegrass Isle]] in the [[Shackles]]. She captains the [[Ship|sloop]] *[[Besmara Winks]]* and is known across the [[Shackles]] for her rare wit, skill with a [[Falchion|falchion]], and her lively manner. Darla is also quite devout and two [[Priest|priests]] of [[Besmara]] serve in her crew: [[Hesla Fornathi]], and the [[Gnome|gnome]] [[Green Jacobo]]. The [[Boatswain|boatswain]] of *Besmara Winks* is the pessimist [[Dwarf|dwarf]] Foul Margot.
