> **Nelris Sargal** is the leader of [[Szamrak's Haven|Szamraks Haven]], a secret [[Android|android]] settlement in [[Numeria]]. She inhabits the same body that the town's founder Szamrak once did. She protects her people with [[Robot|robots]] salvaged or stolen from the [[Technic League]] without hesitation, even from non-androids who simply stumble upon it; those who can participate in its research can stay, but are never allowed to leave lest they expose it to the Technic League.
