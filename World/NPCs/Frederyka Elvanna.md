> **Baroness Frederyka Elvanna** is one of [[Elvanna|Queen Elvannas]] many great-granddaughters. Frederyka and her husband [[Boalloc|Baron Boalloc]] rule the river town of [[Belila]], on the northwestern border of [[Irrisen|Irrisens]] [[Bleakmarch]] province, despite the fact they both prefer to be with their [[Jadwiga|own kind]] in [[Algidheart]] or [[Whitethrone]]. They express their disappointment exercising cruelties on the poor peasants and the servants of their stony keep.
