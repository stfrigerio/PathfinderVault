> **Exemplar Khymrasa** is a wealthy [[Osirion|Osirian]] noble, amateur [[Osirionologist]], and patron of [[Archaeological|archaeological]] excavations in [[Osirion]]. She leads a dig near the ruins of [[Tumen]] in a search for the buried pyramid of [[Ahn'Selota|AhnSelota]].


## Appearance

> Khymrasa is a middle-aged woman of natural beauty. Befitting her wealth and prominence, she wears a striking, though modest, gossamer sari and jewelry made of [[Gold|gold]] and [[Gem|gemstones]].


## Relatives

> Khymrasa's tremendous wealth comes from her family, and she has spent it aggressively seeking [[Artifact|artifacts]] from [[Ancient Osirion|ancient Osirion]].
