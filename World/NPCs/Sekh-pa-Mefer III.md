> During the [[First Age of Osirion]] about 7,500 years ago, **Sekh-pa-Mefer III** ruled [[Osirion]], and is known as the **Pharaoh of Sphinxes**.



## Background

> Sekh-pa-Mefer III is a well-documented friend of [[Sphinx|sphinxes]] that lived in the deserts of Osirion. These creatures were common to see in the public court of the [[Pharaoh|pharaoh]]. This friendship led him to claim that the sphinxes had given him the secret to eternal life, promising that he would return after death to continue to lead the country as a [[Deity|god]]. Unfortunately, his brain was damaged during the mummification process and he rose from the dead as promised, but as a [[Mummy|mummy]], instead of as a deity. ## Relatives

> Sekh-pa-Mefer III had two wives, Bastetabi and Senenetka. Both were buried with him in his tomb.

> As a beloved pharaoh, Sekh-pa-Mefer III was buried with many riches. These expensive items included [[Weapon|weapons]], jewels, furniture and even clothing.
