> **Garzukk** and his sister [[Trelka]] are the two priests who lead the [[Pahmet]] [[Dwarf|dwarves]] of the necropolis of [[Erekrus]] in [[Osirion]]. Together they guide the Pahmet in their prayers and rituals to honor the deceased [[Pharaoh|god-kings]] of Osirion.


## Appearance

> They have disparate features for siblings as Garzukk, the elder of the two, has long golden locks of hair and bronze skin, while Trelka is dour with dusky skin.
