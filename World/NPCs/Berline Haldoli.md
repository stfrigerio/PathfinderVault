> **Berline Haldoli** is a minor [[Blood Lord]] responsible for the [[Zombie|zombie]]-worked farms in the hinterlands of [[Graydirge]] in [[Geb's Rest]]. Though a member of the ruling elite of Geb, Berline has chosen a relatively quiet existence outside the major political center of the nation and is quite comfortable in her position.


## Description

> Berline is a [[Halfling|halfling]] of middle age and has brownish-red, curly hair. She is remarkably, and disturbingly, upbeat considering the macabre nature of her environment and profession.
