> **Adalbert Aspodell**, also known as **Aspodell the Lash**, is a notorious [[Pirate|pirate]] captain who now serves under the [[Pathfinder Society|Pathfinder]] captain [[Challys Argent]] as one of her so-called [[Devil's Four|Devils Four]].


## Appearance

> Once a nobleman, Adalbert Aspodell fights not with a pirates' brutality but with the elegant grace of an aristocratic [[Duelist|duelist]]. Swirling through combat, he uses a [[Rapier|rapier]] rather than the more usual cutlass. Despite this, he still inflicts brutal injuries with the elegant [[Weapon|weapon]], after all, as a member of the Devil's Four, he has a sadistic streak a mile wide.
