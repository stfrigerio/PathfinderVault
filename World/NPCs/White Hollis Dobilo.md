> **White Hollis Dobilo** is the [[Pirate|pirate]] captain of the [[Ship|brig]] *[[Stormrunner]]*, which sails out of [[Drenchport]]. He has secret, lucrative trading deals with matriarch Pelanquelle bringing foodstuffs and supplies in exchange for raw gemstones and rare subterranean substances.
