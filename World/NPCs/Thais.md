> The five-winged **Thais** is the [[Herald|herald]] of the Accidental God, [[Cayden Cailean]]. She normally appears as a 15–foot tall woman with [[Angel|angelic]] features, but can also change her size to be anywhere from 4 to 32 feet. She wields a crystalline [[Halberd|halberd]] called *Tyranny's Foil*, borrowed from the armory of [[Milani]].
