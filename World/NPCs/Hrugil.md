> **Hrugil** was the son of [[Iobar I|King Iobar I]], and the second king of a united [[Iobaria]]. After his father's death at the [[Battle of Kridorn]], he took charge of the new nation, and spent the remainder of his rule improving Iobaria's fleets, with the intent of wiping out the mercenary [[Pirate|pirate]] fleets that plagued the coasts of the northwestern [[Castrovin Sea]].
