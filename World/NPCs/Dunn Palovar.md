> **Chief Fleshforger Dunn Palovar** represents the city of [[Ecanus]] on [[Nex]]'s ruling [[Council of Three and Nine]]. His main rival is [[Borume]], representative of the city of [[Oenopion]]; each is tireless in his attempts to increase his own standing on the Council and reduce that of his opponent.
> Palovar does not attend the Council in person. Instead, he transfers his consciousness into a [[Flesh golem|flesh golem]] which he uses to attend the meetings.
