> **Carellia Thrune** was the fourth member of [[House Thrune|her house]] to hold the throne of [[Cheliax]] following the [[Thrune Ascendancy]]. She inherited rulership from her uncle [[Terthule Thrune|Terthule]] in [[4682 AR]], but drowned under mysterious circumstances that were never investigated properly in [[4696 AR]]. She was succeeded by her cousin, [[Infrexus Thrune|Infrexus]].
