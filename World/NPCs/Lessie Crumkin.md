> **Lessie Crumkin** is the proprietor of [[Trunau Countinghouse]], a bank in the small town of [[Trunau]] in the [[Hold of Belkzen]]. She is a descendant of the bank's founder, [[Barran Crumkin]] and good in her job as a banker. Lessie is also a member of the [[Council of Defenders|town council]] and her skills at arms in the [[Commons Trunau|Commons]] training arena are well known. People say that she is in love with [[Jagrin Grath]].
