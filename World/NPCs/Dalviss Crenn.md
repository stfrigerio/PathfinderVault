> **Dalviss Crenn** is a former [[Adventurer|adventurer]] and the proprietor of the [[Mineshaft]] inn and tavern in the [[Isger|Isgeri]] town of [[Dustpawn]].
> An amateur [[Astronomer|astronomer]], in [[4712 AR]], Dalviss witnessed an [[Lirgen's Glory|object]] falling from the sky and crashing into the Dustpawn hinterlands through his telescope. He doubted local guesses that the object was a [[Meteorite|meteorite]].
> Dalviss is a longtime bachelor. He employs one of his old friends, [[Nalan Hossler]], as the Mineshaft's stabler.
