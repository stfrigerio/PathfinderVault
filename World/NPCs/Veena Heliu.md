> **Veena Heliu** is the dynamic, fire-haired [[Precentors Martial|Precentor Martial for Magic]] in [[Vigil]]. Veena is a nonconformist [[Half-elf|half-elf]] woman, aggressive in both war and her personal affairs. Many in the crusade adore her and many are even in love with her, referring to her as "Our Lady", a title usually reserved for [[Iomedae]] herself.
> Veena Heliu claims her sorcerer powers come from a draconic bloodline and has tried to convince the other Precentors Martial that forging an alliance with [[Dragon|dragons]] would help their mission.
