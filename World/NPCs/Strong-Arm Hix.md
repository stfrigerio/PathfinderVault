> **Strong-Arm Hix** is an old beggar who lives in [[Quent]] in the [[Shackles]]. He has no legs; he claims to have lost them fifty years ago in the [[Cannibal Isles]]. According to Hix, he was part of the crew of the *[[Spindrift Reaver]]*. They landed on the Isles, and all the crew were captured by savage [[Kuru|kuru]] [[Cannibals|cannibals]]. All except Hix were eaten alive as a sacrifice to a [[Deity|deity]] the kuru called the [[Blood Queen]]. Hix escaped in a [[Canoe|canoe]], but lost his legs to the cannibals in the process.
