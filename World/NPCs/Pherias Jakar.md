> Merchant Master **Pherias Jakar** is an important resident of the city of [[Port Peril]], in the [[Shackles]]. She serves as one of the city's joint rulers, along with [[Tsojmin Kreidoros]] and [[Sabas Odabio]], during the frequent absences of [[Pirate lord|pirate lord]] [[Kerdak Bonefist]]. Like the other two, she has a talent for lists and books of accounts, and considerable knowledge of the legal systems of the [[Inner Sea region]].
