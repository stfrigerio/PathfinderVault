> **Jendara Eriksdottir** (who sometimes refers to herself as **Jendara Eriksson**) is the daughter of Erik Eriksson the White; widow of Ikran and mother to [[Kran]]. Once an infamous [[Pirate|pirate]], Jendara is now a merchant travelling around the [[Lands of the Linnorm Kings]] and further afield on the ship *[[Milady]]*, captained by [[Vorrin]].
