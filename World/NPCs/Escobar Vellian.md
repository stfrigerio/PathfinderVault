> **Escobar Vellian** was a warrior-mage from [[Galt]]. He is credited with braving the waterlogged temple of [[Xanthuun]] in the [[Sodden Lands]], battling a horde of drowned [[Zombie|zombies]] to reach the fabled treasure holds of [[Ammelon VI]], last patriarch of the ancient kingdom of [[Ghol-Gan]]. Vellian's exploits were published in *[[Pathfinder ChroniclesVolume 13|Pathfinder Chronicles]]* volume 23.
