> **Jorgyz Erasyn** serves as the so-called "Speaker of the Concord", who speaks for the group of merchants known as the [[Concord of Peers]]. The Concord of Peers are a group that serves as one of the [[Water Lord Ular Kel|Water Lords]] that rule the [[Karazh]] capital of [[Ular Kel]]. They are a group of prominent merchants who control the southernmost of the the three [[Water Houses]] that define the city.
