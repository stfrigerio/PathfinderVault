> Middle-aged **Master Sabas Odabio** is an important resident of the city of [[Port Peril]], in the [[Shackles]]. He serves as the city's joint ruler, along with [[Tsojmin Kreidoros]] and [[Pherias Jakar]], during the frequent absences of [[Pirate lord|pirate lord]] [[Kerdak Bonefist]]. Like the other two, he has a talent for lists and books of account, and considerable knowledge of the legal systems of the [[Inner Sea region]].
