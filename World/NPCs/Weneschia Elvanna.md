> **Duchess Weneschia Elvanna** is the [[Jadwiga]] ruler of the [[Irrisen|Irriseni]] province of [[Bleakmarch]]. She dwells with her consort, the [[Pavel Elvanna|Count Pavel Elvanna]], in her castle called the [[Tiered Keep]] in the regional capital of [[Algidheart]]. Due to her title and position, it is believed that she is a daughter of [[Elvanna|Queen Elvanna]] and was expected to hold her title until [[Baba Yaga|Baba Yagas]] return in [[4713 AR]] to replace Elvanna.
