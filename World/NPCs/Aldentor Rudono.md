> **Aldentor Rudono** is the chief conductor at the prestigious [[Palace of Voices]] in the city of [[Caliphas city|Caliphas]]. Due to the squabbling of the Palace's owners, the brother and sister [[Paulo Carameizta|Paulo]] and [[Marrian Carameizta]], Aldentor has become the *de facto* creative leader of the Palace of Voices. Aldentor is a [[Music|musical]] genius who has kept the theatre-going public enraptured. Aldentor is known to have a love-hate relationship with [[Calmadra Vhalikackos]], the headmistress of the [[Karcau Opera]].
