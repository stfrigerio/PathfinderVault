> The elderly **Benton Grone** had retired from the [[Pathfinder Society]] until the death of his nephew [[Eldis Grone|Eldis]] in [[4709 AR]] caused him to once again become involved. He has continued his nephew's work, studying the [[Jistkan]] ruins of [[Cheliax]], even going so far as to pen a book on the matter: *The Artificers of Rachikan*.


## Background

> After coming out of retirement, Benton began studying the ancient Jistkan ruins of [[Rachikan]], located on Cheliax's southwestern coast. After his good friend [[Drandle Dreng]] lost contact with Benton, Dreng sent a group of [[Pathfinder|Pathfinders]] to Rachikan to rescue him.
