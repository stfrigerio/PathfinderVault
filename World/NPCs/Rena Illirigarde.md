> **Lady Rena Illirigarde** was an [[Ustalav|Ustalavic]] socialite with interests in the [[Occult|occult]]. She commissioned [[Illirigarde Manor]], near [[Lantern Lake]] in the [[Hungry Mountains]] foothills, to study the origins of [[Occult|occult]] phenomena in the mountains.
> A [[Harrowing|harrowing]] she performed for [[Veny Srevka]] in [[4672 AR]] resulted in his death. Soon after, Lady Illirigarde died at the hands of her own [[Homunculus|homunculus]], Andolf.
> As part of her occult research, Lady Illirigarde developed several [[Psychic magic|psychically]] sensitive etheric compasses to track the source of strange phenomena.
