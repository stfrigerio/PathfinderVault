> "Baron" **Venigo Palpathe** is one of the [[Pirate lord|pirate lords]] of [[Raketooth]], the second largest port on [[Shark Island]] in the [[Shackles]]. He also claims that he has shaken hands with the [[Tar-Baphon|Whispering Tyrant]] and other unbelievable things.
