> **Jubrayl Vhiski** leads the [[Sczarni]] in [[Sandpoint]]. Part businessman and part criminal, Jubrayl covertly owns the [[Fatman's Feedbag|Fatmans Feedbag]] and has designs on Sandpoint's brothel business. Jubrayl has managed to evade capture by Sandpoint's sheriff, [[Belor Hemlock]], despite Jubrayl's coordination of a gang of nearly two dozen and the sheriff's strong suspicions. His brother [[Sandru Vhiski]], a former [[Adventurer|adventurer]], is a caravan master whose route includes Sandpoint.
