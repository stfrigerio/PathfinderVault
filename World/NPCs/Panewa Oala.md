> **Panewa Oala** is the portreeve and overseer of [[Queen Bes]] in [[Besmara's Throne|Besmaras Throne]] island. It is said that Panewa was once chased by the [[Taldan Imperial Navy|Taldan Navy]] across the [[Inner Sea]] having plundered a month's worth of pay to the [[Taldan Phalanx|Taldan Army]].
