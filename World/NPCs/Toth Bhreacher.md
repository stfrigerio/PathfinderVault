> **Master Toth Bhreacher** is the owner and operator of the [[Golemworks]] in the [[Bridgeward]] district of the of [[Varisia|Varisian]] city of [[Magnimar]]. Despite his influential position as head of a large and profitable industry, Toth is most famous for his discoveries in [[Magic|magic]]. He discovered that the stone blocks of the [[Irespan]] were far easier to enchant than normal stone, particularly in the creation of [[Construct|constructs]], and capitalized on his discovery to expand the Golemworks, which was originally just his personal studio.
