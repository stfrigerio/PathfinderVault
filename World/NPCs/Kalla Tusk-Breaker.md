> **Kalla Tusk-Breaker** is one of the councilors of the [[Half-orc|half-orc]] city of [[Averaka]].  She strongly believes in the importance of a city for half-orcs.  She is the military leader in Averaka, controlling the Averaka town guard and was once one of [[Estrid the White]]'s captains.  She is eager for a half-orc to become a [[Linnorm King]].
