> **Paravicar Acillmar** is one of the most senior members of the [[Hellknights]] [[Order of the Nail]]. He is the leader of the order's [[Hellknight ranks|signifers]], the [[Spellcaster|spellcasters]] of a Hellknight order.
> Acillmar is described as a ruddy, red-haired man with a lithe frame. He is known to be a 'servant of propriety', expecting respect, decorum and an adherence to protocol from others. He normally resides in [[Paravicar's Tower|Paravicars Tower]] in [[Citadel Vraid]].
> As a member of the Order of the Nail, Acillmar shares their goal of beating back degenerate savagery in all its forms to make way for the expansion of civilization.
