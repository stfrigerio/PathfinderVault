> **Brantos Calderon** is a [[Paladin|paladin]] of [[Iomedae]] in in the small town of [[Trunau]] in the [[Hold of Belkzen]]. 
Formerly stationed at [[Castle Firrine]], he forsook his post to pledge his blade (and some say his heart) to the resolute young cleric [[Tyari Varvatos]] who fights on this harsh frontier.
