> **Blondie Humbe** is the first mate on the [[Ship|three-masted barque]] *[[Bold Folly]]*, captained by [[Bent-Beak Charney]], one of the three captains of the [[Firegrass Isle]] in the [[Shackles]]. Named for her elaborate, straw-yellow braids, Blondie Humbe is famous in taverns across the Shackles for her amusing art of [[Curse|cursing]].
