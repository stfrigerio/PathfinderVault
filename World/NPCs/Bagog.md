>  [[Half-orc]] **Bagog** is the owner of [[Bagog's Roundhouse]] tavern in the town of [[Dragonsthrall]] on [[Dahak's Horn]] island in the [[Shackles]]. Bagog has been left blind due to his prolonged exposure to the fumes of the still in which he makes his famous [[Bagog's liquor]]. His [[liquor]] is infamous for the speed at which it intoxicates and the deleterious effects of its regular consumption.
