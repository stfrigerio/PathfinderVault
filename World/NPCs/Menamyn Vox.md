> **Menamyn Vox** is tribal leader of the [[Swifthooves]], a [[PathfinderWiki/Geography/Cities/Centaur|centaur]] tribe that roams the [[Egobarius Plain]] in [[Cheliax]].
> She had been known to take in and protect victims of bandit attacks, but otherwise prefers to avoid the Chelish authorities and focus on matters of tradition and inter-tribal politics.
> Her sister, [[Maidrayne Vox]], left the tribe to join the [[Order of the Nail]].
