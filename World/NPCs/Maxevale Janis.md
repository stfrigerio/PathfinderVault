> **Maxevale Janis** is the [[Pirate lord|pirate lord]] of the [[Rapier Bay]], a rowdy small town on [[Motaku Isle]] in the [[Shackles]]. He is captain of the [[Ship|galleon]] *[[Motaku Maiden]]*. He has led Rapier Bay since the death of his predecessor in [[4706 AR]]. Maxevale always supports [[Tessa Fairwind|Lady Tessa]] in the [[Pirate Council]], earning the nickname "the Puppy", though no man dares to say it to his face.
