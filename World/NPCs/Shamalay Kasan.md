> **Shamalay Kasan** is the high priestess of the [[Child-goddess]] and dwells in the neighborhood of [[Ankar-Te]], within the [[Varisia|Varisian]] city of [[Kaer Maga]]. Because of her great influence, she is considered one of the most powerful leaders with the district, and often shares dispute-settlement and arbitration duties with [[Horus Ilaktya]].
