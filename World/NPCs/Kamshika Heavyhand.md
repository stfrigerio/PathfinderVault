> **Kamshika Heavyhand** is one of the four [[Pirate|pirates]] in the small pirate settlement of [[Yelligo Wharf]] on the island of [[Dahak's Tooth|Dahaks Tooth]] in the [[Shackles]]. She captains the armored [[Ship|sloop]] *[[Baleful Glare]]*. These four captains capture [[Inner Sea]] merchant vessels on their way home from [[Port Peril]], stealing their cargoes, and selling their crews to the cultists of [[Dahak]] for sacrifice.
