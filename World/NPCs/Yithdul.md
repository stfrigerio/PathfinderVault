> **Yithdul** was a [[Sorcerer|sorcerer]] prince and [[Arcane|arcane]] diplomat of the ancient [[Shory]] flying city of [[Ulduvai]]. The weakest of the three people corrupted by a strange, otherworldly stone (along with [[Yakmar]] and [[Banderak]]), Yithdul became an [[Undead|undead]] dread wight after their actions activated the stone and caused Ulduvai to fall from the skies. Unlike his companions, Yithdul remained sane through his transformation.
> Yithdul can command some [[Invisible stalker|invisible stalkers]] as well as a [[Belker|belker]] named [[Aethsan]].


## Early life

> Yithdul was a diplomat for Ulduvai and married to an unknown woman.
