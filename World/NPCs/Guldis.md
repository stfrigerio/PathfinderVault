> Emir **Guldis** is the young ruler of [[Pashow]], one of the five allied city-states of [[Thuvia]]. The city has lost its last two shipments of [[Sun orchid elixir|sun orchid elixir]] to teleportation accidents. The cause of these mishaps is unknown, but the citizens hold Guldis responsible. He is desperately seeking to solve the mystery and restore his reputation.
> In the meantime, a group of agitators are seeking overthrow Guldis and put [[Ziralia]], eldest daughter of Prince [[Zinlo]] of [[Aspenthar]], on the throne of Pashow.
