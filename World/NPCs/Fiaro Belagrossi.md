> **Fiaro Belagrossi** is an aged member of the [[Order of Cyphers]] who claims descent from the first [[Varisian|Varisians]]. He currently runs an information network from [[Magnimar]] who tracks down the clandestine trade of [[Thassilon|Thassilonian]] relics by potentially dangerous owners of Thassilonian [[Magic|magic]]. He shares his goals with the city's wealthy collectors, who often provide him with information and [[Money|money]].
