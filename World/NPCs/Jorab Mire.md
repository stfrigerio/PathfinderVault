> **Consul Marshal Jorab Mire** is the current head of the [[Andoren]] armed forces, and is headquartered in the city of [[Carpenden]]. As a consul, he advises the [[Supreme Elect]] on military matters, but his more immediate duty is to oversee the armed forces of Andoran, nearly a third of whom are stationed in Carpenden.
