> **Mishea Lessina**, and her husband **Alvanore** are the innkeepers of the popular Savage Vine inn in [[Cesca]], a small town in county [[Varno]], [[Ustalav]]. Prior to this role Mishea was a [[Vintner|vintner]], and evidence of her former job is found in the inn. She enjoys mixing with her clientele, especially if loud and hearty folk singing kicks off or new tales of strange occurrences are told.
