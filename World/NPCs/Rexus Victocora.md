> **Rexus Victocora** is a scholar and philosopher in the [[Cheliax|Chelish]] city of [[Kintargo]] and a noble of [[House Victocora]].



## Appearance

> Rexus is a handsome, dusky-skinned young man with dark hair, a goatee, and fine clothing. He is of a short, slight build and always carries a cane.


## Relatives

> Born to Aneran and [[Porcia Victocora]], he was raised as a lady in Kintargo's noble circles until a meeting with a [[Shelyn|Shelynite]] tutor proficient in changing genders. This meeting inspired him to transition into a man through [[Alchemy|alchemical]] unguents which, even with his parents' support, resulted in scandal, ostracization, and threats.
> His parents have been missing and presumed dead since [[House Thrune]] attacked Kintargo during the [[Night of Ashes]] and burned his family's estate in [[The Greens]] in [[4715 AR]]. After witnessing the aftermath of the attack, he went into hiding in Kintargo.


## Associates

> Family friend [[Laria Longroad]] took Rexus in after House Thrune's attack.


## Background

> Rexus studied at Kintargo's [[Alabaster Academy]].
