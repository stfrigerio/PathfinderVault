> **Jirukarakaza** is one of the oldest and the most powerful of the [[Underworld dragon|underworld dragons]] who dwell in the molten caves beneath the [[Volcano|volcanic]] Mount Yogan in [[Minkai]]. Over the years, he has raided numerous caravans, accumulated a huge hoard and established a formidable reputation among his neighbours. Recently, he has been relying on the dominion of the [[Soto Takahiro|Jade Regent]] to extend his domain, and has his eyes on [[Hiyosai]], a thriving mining and trade town.
