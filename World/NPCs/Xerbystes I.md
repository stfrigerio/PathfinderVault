> **Xerbystes I** was a historical [[Satrap|satrap]] of [[Qadira]], who ruled the nation in the mid-16th century [[Absalom Reckoning|AR]]. He is best known from turning his position of satrap into a hereditary title. In exchange, Xerbystes I gave up control over Qadira's foreign affairs, which were thereafter handled by a [[Vizier|vizier]] who was appointed by the [[Padishah Emperor]]. Xerbystes I also signed an historic peace agreement with [[Urios III|Grand Prince Urios III]] of Taldor, beginning the [[Urian Peace]] that lasted for over 2,500 years.
