> **Thuurv** is an unkempt [[Half-orc|half-orc]] and [[Venture-captain|venture-captain]] in the [[Pathfinder Society]]. His intimidating appearance helps conceal a sharp intellect.
> His current assignment is within [[Galt]] in the city of [[Woodsedge]]. The [[Decemvirate]] instructed Thuurv to reclaim the ruined [[Woodsedge Lodge]], find information on any stolen [[Artifact|artifacts]], and avenge the [[Pathfinder]] murdered in the riots of [[4704 AR]].
> To escape detection from the revolutionaries and continue the uncover operation, Thuurv poses as a gate-guard for one of the city's few surviving nobles.
> Thuurv works alongside two other operatives, the elderly ex-adventurer [[Lofton]] and [[Sorcerer|sorceress]] [[Senri Stenn]]. However, Thuurv is officially in charge of the group. The trio sponsors Pathfinders on missions to reclaim lost treasures and explore nearby lands.
> They base their operations in the hidden basements of the so-called [[Barren Museum]].
