> **Baron Boalloc** is the husband of [[Frederyka Elvanna|Baroness Frederyka Elvanna]], one of [[Elvanna|Queen Elvannas]] many great-granddaughters, and ruler of the river town [[Belila]] on the northwestern border of [[Irrisen|Irrisens]] [[Bleakmarch]] province. Baroness and Baron both would prefer to be with their own kind in [[Algidheart]] or [[Whitethrone]]. They express their disappointment exercising cruelties on the poor peasants and the servants of their stony keep.
