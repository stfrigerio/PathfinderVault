> **Lathroft** was the second most powerful of the [[Shadow wizard|shadow wizards]]. He was killed by [[Elyana Sadrastis]] when she and her adventuring party of [[Stelan]], [[Arcil]], [[Vallyn]], [[Daramont]], and [[Eriah]] tracked the shadow wizard to his lair in the shadow wizards' library fortress, a feat which had taken nearly a year. In this epic battle Daramont fell to a summoned [[Umbral dragon|umbral dragon]].
> He had a niece called [[Nadara]], who was present at her uncle's final demise.
> He had owned a *[[Ring of shadow walking|ring of shadow walking]]*, which was taken by Elyana Sadrastis when the spoils of their victory over Lathroft were shared out.
