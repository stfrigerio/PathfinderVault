> **Amitrie Leth** lives alongside her seven sisters in a forest within the *[[Dancing Hut of Baba Yaga]]*, where they reside inside a structure woven out of [[Silk|silk]] high in the branches of a giant oak. They were given near-[[Immortality|immortality]] by [[Baba Yaga]] at an unknown point in the past, and have lived within her home for much longer than araneas normally live.
> Amitrie is a devoted follower of [[Nethys]], and dedicates her time to researching the nature of the [[Universe|universe]]. She is particularly interested in [[History|history]], [[Astronomy|astronomy]], and [[Magic|magic]]. Amitrie and her sisters conduct their research both through personally studying the locations and phenomena they encounter through the *Dancing Hut'*s [[Planar travel|planar travels]], and through trading information and [[Magic item|magic items]] with travelers passing through their home.
