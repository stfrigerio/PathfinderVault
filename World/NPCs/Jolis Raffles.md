> **Jolis Raffles** is a [[Pirate lord|pirate lord]] who rules [[Bag Island]] in the [[Shackles]]. He is captain of the [[Galley|galley]] *[[Chains of Freedom]]*.
> Raffles was once a galley [[Slave|slave]], and he attacks only the slave [[Ship|ships]] of [[Cheliax]]. He offers the slaves of the ships he captures the chance to settle on Bag Island. It is rumoured that he funds his operations by selling those slaves who turn down his offer.
