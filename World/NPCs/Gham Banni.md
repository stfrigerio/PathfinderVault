> **Gham Banni** is a [[Venture-captain|venture-captain]] in the [[Pathfinder Society]] operating out of [[Osirion]]. Of the many [[Pathfinder|Pathfinders]] reporting to him, the most notable is perhaps [[Channa Ti]], the [[Half-elf|half-elf]] [[Rules/Classes/Druid|druid]] who sought the lost city of Xanchara in [[4709 AR]]. He is known for using a soft green papyrus and [[Ring|magical signet ring]] to sign his correspondences.
