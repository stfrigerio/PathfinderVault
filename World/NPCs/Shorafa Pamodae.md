> An alluring but dangerous [[Tiefling|tiefling]], **Shorafa Pamodae** is the high priestess of [[Calistria]] in the [[God|goddess]]'s [[Riddleport]] temple, the [[House of the Silken Veil]]. She acts as a madam to the prostitutes working at the temple, and uses these girls as sources of information, which she later sells. She is considered the city's major information broker, which led to her ascension to become one of the city's minor crimebosses.
> A female tiefling who is always seen clad in leather and carries a snakeskin [[Whip|whip]] coiled around her shoulder, Shorafa also runs the hospitality industry in the city. Everyone from pimps to [[Alcoholic beverages|liquor]] sellers answer to Shorafa, and she is considered the ruler of the "hospitality" industry in Riddleport.
