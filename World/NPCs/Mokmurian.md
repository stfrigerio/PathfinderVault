> Self-styled **Lord Mokmurian** was the most notorious of the bandit chiefs broken away from the mould of [[Stone giant|stone giant]] norms. Until his death, he ruled the fortress of Jorgenfist in the [[Valley of the Black Tower]] in the [[Iron Peaks]], and commanded at least seven stone giant tribes, as well as associated [[Ettin|ettins]], [[Hill giant|hill giants]], [[Ogre|ogres]], and [[Troll|trolls]].
