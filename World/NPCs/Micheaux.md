> **Grand Prince Micheaux the Magnificent** was [[Grand Prince]] of [[Taldor]] from [[4499 AR]] to [[4526 AR]]. He succeeded the childless [[Beldam II]], who adopted Micheaux a year before dying in [[4499 AR]]. Before his adoption, Micheaux had been a powerful Taldan nobleman.
> Micheaux was married to Euphemi Finara and had a son, [[Stavian I|Stavian]], on 5th [[Lamashan]], [[4497 AR]]. Stavian I would succeed Micheaux as Grand Prince in 4526 AR.
