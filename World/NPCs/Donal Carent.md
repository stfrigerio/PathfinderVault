> **Donal Carent** is the [[Varisian human ethnicity|Varisian]] leader of the [[Wreckwash Blades]], a [[Sczarni]] gang run out of [[Carent's Camp|Carents Camp]] in the [[Varisia|Varisian]] city of [[Magnimar]]. He masquerades as a cobbler, but spends most of his days and nights overseeing the members of his gang who specialize in the gathering and trade of information within the city. He recently forced out another Sczarni gang from his camp named the [[Tower Girls]] after a disagreement developed between the two criminal organizations.
