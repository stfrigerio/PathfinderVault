> **Tsojmin Kreidoros** is the bald and humourless harbourmaster of [[Port Peril|Port Perils]] [[Crescent Harbor]]. He also serves as the city's joint ruler, along with [[Sabas Odabio]] and [[Pherias Jakar]], during the frequent absences of [[Pirate lord|pirate lord]] [[Kerdak Bonefist]]. Like the other two, he has a talent for lists and books of account, and considerable knowledge of the legal systems of the [[Inner Sea region]].
