> **Kragreth-Kol** (or **Krageth-Kol**) is a[[PathfinderWiki/Classes/Barbarian|barbarian]]] lord of the Black Horses tribe in the [[Kellid]] nation of [[Numeria]]. A bitter, jealous man, he is a cousin of the [[Black Sovereign]] [[Kevoth-Kul]], Numeria's ruler. He has recently begun to breed [[Vexgit|vexgit]] [[Gremlin|gremlins]] to disrupt and undermine Kevoth-Kul and his [[Technology|technology-dependent]] [[Technic League]] allies, but lacks the means to control them or smuggle them inside [[Starfall]].
