> Captain **Colvaas**, of the [[Ship|sloop]] *[[Island Wench]]*, was a notorious [[Pirate|pirate]].
> He founded a port in the [[Shackles Mainland|Mainland]] region of the [[Shackles]] which he used as a base for his small pirate fleet. He was eventually hunted down and hanged from a gibbet in the town's harbour. His bones still hang there, and today the settlement is known as [[Colvaas Gibbet]].
