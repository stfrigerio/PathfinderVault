> **Constanza Purgote** is the [[Pirate|pirate]] captain of the fast [[Ship|sloop]] *[[Constanza's Whim|Constanzas Whim]]*, which sails out of [[Port Peril]]. Constanza Purgote is one of the handful of captains that know of [[Heslandaena]], the [[Drow|drow]] settlement in the depths of [[Dahak's Tooth|Dahaks Tooth]] island in the [[Shackles]]. She has secret, lucrative trading deals with matriarch Pelanquelle bringing foodstuffs and supplies in exchange for raw gemstones and rare subterranean substances.
