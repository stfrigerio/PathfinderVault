> **Ukabalimeli the Manslayer** is the chieftain of the [[Graveborn]] tribe of [[Eloko]] in the eastern [[Mwangi Expanse]] town of [[Elokolobha]]. Each male member of his tribe must survive a grueling rite of passage in which the youth must survive potent [[Poison|toxins]] and dig his way out of a grave.
