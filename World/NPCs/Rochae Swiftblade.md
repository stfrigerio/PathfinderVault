> The experienced warrior **Rochae Swiftblade** was the Knight Lord of the [[Tempering Hall]]—a training facility for [[Paladin|paladins]] called by [[Iomedae]] as well as by [[Abadar]], [[Irori]], and [[Shelyn]]—located in the [[Ascendant Court]] district of [[Absalom]]. Originally a [[Cleric|cleric]] of the Inheritor, Rochae became a paladin in later years. As Knight Lord, he was succeeded by [[Evandor Malik]].
