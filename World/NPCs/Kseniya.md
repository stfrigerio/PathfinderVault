> **Kseniya** is a daughter of [[Baba Yaga]]. She was the eleventh Queen of [[Irrisen]]. She reigned from [[4313 AR]] until [[4413 AR]], when she was removed from [[Golarion]] and her sister [[Betyrina]] was placed on the throne. Queen Kseniya established the [[Bloodstone Conservatory]] in [[Czarny Las]] in [[4378 AR]].
