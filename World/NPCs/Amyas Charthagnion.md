> **Amyas Charthagnion** is a young [[Human|human]] man and fourth or fifth child of [[House Charthagnion]], a [[Noble houses of Cheliax|Chelish noble house]] based in [[Absalom]]. In [[4719 AR]], he joined with several childhood friends from rival houses — [[Sarevi Tilernos]], [[Jasper Caperina]], [[Leonie Ciucci]], and [[Dacian Julistarc]] — on an expedition to the [[Menador Mountains]]. Amyas has bright red hair, wears expensive scarlet-dyed clothing, and carries himself with flamboyance and confident leadership.
