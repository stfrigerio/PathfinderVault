> **Klarkosh**, the clockwork crafter and self-proclaimed master of the [[Emerald Spire]], is a [[Numeria|Numerian]] [[Wizard|wizard]], [[Engineer|engineer]], and [[Artificer|artificer]] who has settled into the spire's upper six levels. He is a [[Technic League]] initiate, and through his creation and control of automated [[Clockwork|clockwork]] [[Emerald automaton|emerald automatons]] he has subjugated many of the upper levels' creatures. This includes the [[Bugbear|bugbear]] Grulk who guards the spire's entrance in the Echo Woods, and the bone priest Gorloth. Klarkosh himself resides on the sixth level, also known as the Clockwork Maze.


## Appearance

> Klarkosh is a tall man clad in [[Gold|gold]], and looks as much like a machine as he does a [[Human|human]].
