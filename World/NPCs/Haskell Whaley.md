> **Consul Admiral Haskell Whaley** is the head of the [[Andoran|Andoren]] navy and an elected member of the [[People's Council|Peoples Council]], that nation's governing body. His headquarters are in [[Fort Constance]] in the major port city of [[Augustana]]. Due to increased [[Pirate|piracy]] on the upper [[Sellen River]], Whaley has recently increased the number of privateer's writs his office issues.
