> **Moxix** is a unique and powerful [[Demon|demon]] native to the island of [[Yoha's Graveyard|Yohas Graveyard]] in the [[Shackles]], and has lived there for a very long time. Also known as the **Drinker of Human Hopes**, he gained this moniker during the time of the ancient [[Cyclopes|cyclopes]] empire of [[Ghol-Gan]], when the one-eyed giants used to sacrifice [[Human|humans]] to him.
