> **Lady Madrona Daugustana** is the matriarch of the family of Daugustanas in [[Anthusis|Eleder]] and the unofficial city's leader. She is the oldest living colonial and she upholds the ideals and customs of her ancestors. She is a member of the high council of the city and her political influence stretches all the way to [[Baron's Palace|Barons Palace]]. She controls almost one third of all major businesses in the port. All activities in [[Portside Eleder district|Portside]] and [[New Haliad]], from marriages to business deals must have her approval.
