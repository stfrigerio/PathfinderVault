> **Seferin** is a  [[Cloud giant|cloud giant]] who lives in the [[Fenwall Mountains]] in [[Varisia]]. He descends from the mountains every three years and visits the sleepy mining town of [[Baslwief]] for a cooking contest. He offers a jewel the size of a chicken egg for the finest dish and, [[Currency|platinum coins]] for any that please him. Fine chefs from all corners of Varisia come to Baslwief for the famous contest.
