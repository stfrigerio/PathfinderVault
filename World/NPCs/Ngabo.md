> **Ngabo the Deathbringer** is the chieftain of the [[Ironbell]] tribe of [[Eloko]] in the eastern [[Mwangi Expanse]] town of [[Elokolobha]], located on the boundary of the [[Screaming Jungle]]. The Ironbell tribe is famous for its use of small [[Iron|iron]] bells, which its members ring to lure prey to their deaths.
