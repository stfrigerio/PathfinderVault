> **Captain Almira "Cardinal" Perine** is a captain of the Ustalavic Navy who is based out of [[Castle Golbanze]] in the city of [[Caliphas city|Caliphas]]. She serves beneath [[Marris Riddian|Admiral Marris Riddian]] and her duties are to protect the people of Caliphas and all those who live along [[Ustalav|Ustalavs]] coast from [[Pirate|pirates]] and aquatic monsters from [[Lake Encarthan]], as well as dealing with the occasional raider from nearby [[Razmiran]].
