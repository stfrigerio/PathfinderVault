> **Tragshi the Herbalist** is the chief poisoner, or "Guildmistress", of the [[Daggermark Poisoners' Guild|Daggermark Poisoners Guild]].


## Description

> Tragshi is an elderly [[Half-elf|half-elven]] woman with a lilting voice and golden-tan skin. She has not always lived in [[Daggermark]], but came from the east. She is always accompanied by dozens of venomous [[PathfinderWiki/Creatures/Snake|snakes]] which slither beneath her robes.
