> **Gerik Mubb** is a [[Cleric|cleric]] of [[Urgathoa]]  who lives in the [[Varisia|Varisian]] city of [[Kaer Maga]].  He runs [[Reanimations]], in the [[Ankar-Te]] [[Ring district]] of the city, and bitterly resents the greater success of the rival [[Last Rites]].
> Mubb supplies mindless [[Undead|undead]], known as the [[Twice-Born]]; his items are cheaper than those of Last Rites, but of a lower quality. He also provides [[Potion|potions]] and [[Cleric|clerical]] services.
