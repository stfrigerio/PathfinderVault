> **Mistress Ayamyra** is the current head of the [[Dome of the Savored Sting]], [[Calistria|Calistrias]] temple in the [[Varisia|Varisian]] city of [[Magnimar]]. She personally oversees the church's revenge business (a service to enact retribution for pay), delighting in finding the right punishment for every crime. She is a young-looking [[Elf|elven woman]] with shoulder-length, dark hair, whose long ears sport countless piercings.
