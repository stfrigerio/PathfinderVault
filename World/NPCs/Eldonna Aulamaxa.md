> **Archbaroness Eldonna Aulamaxa** is the head of the [[Kintargo|Kintargan]] aristocratic [[House Aulamaxa]]. Politically, her loyalties lie more with Kintargo than [[Cheliax]] and the ruling [[House Thrune]]. Her House is a member of the influential [[Court of Coin]] and her House interests include the [[Kintargo Opera House|opera]], hunting and swaying popular opinion.
