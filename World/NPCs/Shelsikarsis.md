> **Shelsikarsis** is an ancient [[White dragon|white dragon]] who dwells near [[Citadel Cardekriel]] on the [[Plane of Air|Elemental Plane of Air]]. Shelsikarsis serves as the leader of an army of white dragons that almost constantly besiege Citadel Cardekriel, where they seek the *[[Shastiled]]* a set of powerful [[Artifact|artifacts]] that Shelsikarsis believes will grant them great power over all other [[Dragon|dragons]]. Shelsikarsis constantly invents new schemes and plots to try to get one of her agents into the well-defended citadel but has yet to outwit [[Pratinaxis]], the [[Silver dragon|silver dragon]] who commands Citadel Cardekriel.
