> **Pharaoh Menedes I**, known as the **Sky Pharaoh**, was the first ruler of the last native dynasty of [[Osirion|Osirian]] pharaohs prior to the [[Qadira|Qadiran]] invasion in [[1532 AR]]. His likeness adorns the [[Sphinx Head|Black Sphinx]] in the desert west of [[Shiman-Sekh]], and the sphinx contains his royal tomb.
