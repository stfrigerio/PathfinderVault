> **Kyda** was a[[Bard's End|bard]]], one of the members of the original [[Silver Ravens]] and the co-creator of the "Song of Silver" alongside the [[Cleric|cleric]] [[Amyreid]]. Kyda was a devout worshipper of [[Shelyn]] whom she claims inspired the "Song of Silver". She composed the "Song of Silver" as part of her duty as a Silver Raven to help defend [[Kintargo]] during the [[Cheliaxhistory|Chelish Civil War]]. She was slain in [[4640 AR]] by the [[Pit fiend|pit fiend]] Oughorthan in the [[Kintargo Opera House]] whilst she performed the "Song of Silver".
