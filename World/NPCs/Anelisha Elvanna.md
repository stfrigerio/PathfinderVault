> **Duchess Anelisha Elvanna** is a [[White Witch]], and the [[Jadwiga]] ruler of the [[Irrisen]] province of [[Hoarwood province|Hoarwood]]. She holds court from her capital city of [[Hoarwood]], located within a massive carved-out oak tree. Prone to numerous debaucheries, she has taken her brother [[Ghrathis]] as her consort.
