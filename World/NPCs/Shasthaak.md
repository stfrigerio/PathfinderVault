> **Shasthaak** was a [[Rune giant|rune giant]] particularly loyal to the [[Runelords|runelords]], who chose him to assassinate [[Xin|First King Xin]]. He was [[Teleportation|teleported]] into Xin's Crystal Palace and wreaked havoc, but as Shasthaak struck the killing blow, Xin pulled out a shard of the *[[Sihedron]]*, resulting in an explosion that annihilated himself, Shasthaak, and his palace alike. Shasthaak's [[Soul|soul]] remained, neither dead nor [[Undead|undead]], still determined to kill Xin.
