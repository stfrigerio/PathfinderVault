> **Lady Arodeth**, a citizen of [[Absalom]], plays the role of the inheritor of a band of mercenaries called the Band of Blades. In reality Lady Arodeth acts as a front for the [[Harbingers of Fate]]. She is a cunning politician and strategist determined to see one of the prophecies of the *[[Book of 1000 Whispers]]* fulfilled.
