> **Cenabal the Crone** is one of the three otherworldly prophets, known as [[Keeba's Eyes|Keebas Eyes]], who lives in the [[Cauldrons of Calm and Calamity]], a series of mystical caves at the foot of [[Mount Keeba]] on the [[Smoker]] island. The three prophets are famous in the [[Shackles]] for their wisdom and oracular prowess.
