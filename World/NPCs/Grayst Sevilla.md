> **Grayst Sevilla** is a [[Varisian]] thug from [[Sandpoint]] who is currently being held in [[Habe's Sanatorium|Habes Sanatorium]]. He was apprehended and is suspected in the brutal murders of three known con men ([[Tarch Mortwell]], [[Lener Hask]], and [[Gedwin Tabe]]) near [[Cougar Creek]]. However, he is also a victim - Sevilla was with the men when they were captured and killed by the [[Skinsaw Man]]. Sevilla went insane from the spectacle and was infected with [[Ghoul fever|ghoul fever]] by the Skinsaw Man along with a message for his obsession.
