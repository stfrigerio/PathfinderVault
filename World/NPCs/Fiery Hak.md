> **Fiery Hak** is a former [[Pirate|pirate]] who now serves as the mayor of [[New Vedagad]], the largest city of the storied region of [[The Cradle]] in [[Vudra]]. New Vedagad is not just large but, thanks to a direct road all the way to the Vudran capital [[Indapatta]], incredibly rich. Fiery Hak, has to constantly deal with surrounding [[Mahajanapada|mahajanapadas]] and scoundrels of all sorts trying to syphon off some of the vast wealth of his city.
