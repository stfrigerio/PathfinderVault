> **Pharaoh An-Hepsu VII** also known as the **Pharaoh of Blades** ruled [[Osirion|Ancient Osirion]] in the -24th century before [[Aroden|Arodens ascension]]. He is remembered for having built the city of [[Ipeq]] using the wish powers of bound [[Genie|genies]] in [[-2385 AR]]. He was ultimately killed by his own son, and his [[Bestiary/Ghost|ghost]] still haunts his tomb (*see [[Tomb of the Pharaoh of Blades]]*).
> The Pharaoh of Blades was famous for his collection of swords, which included legendary pieces like the famous *[[Jeratakra]]*, made by [[Tsing Xiao|Swordmaster Tsing Xiao]], and *[[Harosteir]]*, a [[Golem|golem]]-commanding relic stolen from [[Jistka Imperium|Jistka]].
