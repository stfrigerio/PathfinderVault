> **Liisu Nyetesky** is the owner of the [[Tin King Alley Tavern]] in the small town of [[Zlatomesto]] in [[Irrisen|Irriseni]] province of [[Wintercrux]]. The tavern is famous for the [[Alcoholic beverages|potato vodka]] it serves. Liisu Nyetesky is a secret agent of the underground movement, called the [[Heralds of Summer's Return|Heralds of Summers Return]]. The information she gathers from the drunken patrons concerning the schedule of the most important shipments headed east is valuable for the organization in order to set up ambushes.
