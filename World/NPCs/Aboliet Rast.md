> The **Paralictor Aboliet Rast** is a [[Hellknight ranks|signifer]] member of the [[Hellknights|Hellknight]] [[Order of the Nail]], stationed in [[Citadel Vraid]] outside the [[Varisia|Varisian]] city-state of [[Korvosa]]. There he works in the [[Hellfast]], the secretive, underground complex where the order's more specialized rituals are conducted, and is assisted by an [[Erinys|erinys]] [[Devil|devil]] who also serves as the chapel guardian. In addition, he leads the other Hellknights in services, where he rails against the dangers of [[Infernal|infernal]] possession.
