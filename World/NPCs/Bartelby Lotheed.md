> Count **Bartelby Lotheed** is the eldest son of deceased Count Mercater Lotheed and administrator of the County of Meratt in [[Taldor|Taldan]]. He also serves as tribune for the town of [[Lotheedar]] and landgrave of the Hyden Marches. He remains the sole unmarried male of his family.
> Bartelby is described as tall, thin, and handsome combined with a sharp mind. He has a great interest in the arcane arts. As its steward, he spends most of his time in the [[Palace of Birdsong]] and delegates much of his responsibilities to others particularly his seneschal, Sir [[Gul Gusairne]].
