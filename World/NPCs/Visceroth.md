> **Visceroth** is a powerful [[Umbral dragon|umbral dragon]]. He makes his home in the deeper regions of the [[Shadow Caverns]] in [[Nidal]], where he is served by a number of [[Troglodyte|troglodyte]] minions. The [[Dragon|dragon]] reveres [[Zon-Kuthon]], and is tasked by Nidal's [[Umbral Court]] with regulating the nation's trade—mainly [[Slavery|slaves]]—with the [[Dark folk|dark folk]] living in Rauhlos, a city below the Shadow Caverns.
