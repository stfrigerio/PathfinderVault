> **Suota Iron-Tongue** is one of the three councilors that rule the [[Half-orc|half-orc]] town of [[Averaka]] in the [[Lands of the Linnorm Kings]]. She is the official keeper of the town's lore, mentor of the local [[Art|artists]], and moderator of the annual [[Orcmoot]]. Suota's home holds a great library and she even organizes weekly reading sessions to inspire and educate half-orc children.
