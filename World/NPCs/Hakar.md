> **General Hakar** is the commander of [[Fort Ramgate]], a keep on the borderlands between [[Molthune]] and [[Nirmathas]]. Hakar is a [[Kellid]]-born barbarian who rose to prominence in Molthune's [[Imperial Army]]. The fort is under frequent attack from Nirmathi guerrillas, and Hakar keeps it in a constant state of repair.
