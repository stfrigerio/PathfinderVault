> **Dirimalia VI, the Ninefold Matriarch of Holy Sublimation** is the ruler of the [[Fire mephit|fire mephits]] of the Elemental [[Plane of Fire]]. She lives in the tall central tower of the [[Ninefold Towers of the Matriarch of Holy Sublimation]].
> Her people treat her as a living [[Deity|deity]], and she lives in luxury that even the sultans of the [[Efreeti|efreet]] cannot match. She passes her time in hedonism (she has a multi-species harem), [[Alchemy|alchemy]] and diplomacy.
> Under her leadership, the fire mephits have a secret alliance with the [[Shaitan|shaitans]] of the [[Plane of Earth]].
