> **Pharaoh Jetrieti I**, the **Masked Pharaoh**, ruled [[Osirion|Ancient Osirion]] at the beginning of the -30th century AR. He succeeded the popular and powerful [[Song Pharaoh]], whom he himself killed in [[-2999 AR]]. His rule was the beginning of a long, slow decline for his country.


## Burial

> Jetrieti I was entombed in the [[Pyramid of Masks]] in the [[Tremor Valley]], becoming the first burial site in what is now known as the [[Gozarin Necropolis]].
