> The albino [[World/Geography/Mountains & Hills/Gnoll|gnoll]] **Nathrek the Pale, Devourer of Virgins**, is the nominal chieftain of the gnoll cult based at [[Lamashtu's Flower|Lamashtus Flower]] in [[Osirion]]. In truth, the gnolls fear his consort [[Alashra]] more.
> Nathrek has a daughter from his relationship with Alashra: [[Najak]], the gnoll leader of the [[Marrow Reavers]] adventuring group.
