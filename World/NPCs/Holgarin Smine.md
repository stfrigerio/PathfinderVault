> **Holgarin Smine** is a [[Dwarf|dwarven]] [[Venture-captain|venture-captain]] of the [[Pathfinder Society]] stationed in [[Tymon]].
> Smine operates the [[Smine's Weaponworks|Smines Weaponworks]] in Tymon, which he established over 40 years ago near the [[Arena of Aroden]]. The [[Weapon|weapons]] he produces (referred to as a "Smine") are greatly valued and widely considered a symbol of good luck. He has three undersmiths who aid him in the forging process.
> Smine loves stories involving heroism, hidden tombs, and forgotten treasure. It is said that he knows a lot about the ruined forest fortress of [[Gensmaren]] in [[Razmiran]].
