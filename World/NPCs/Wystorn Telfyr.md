> The [[Pathfinder]] **Wystorn Telfyr** is an advisor to [[Venture-Captain]] [[Brackett]] of the [[Pathfinder lodge]] within the [[Cathedral of Aroden]] in [[Almas]], [[Andoran]]. He is responsible for maintaining the lodge's library.
> Wystorn is a learned sage obsessed with the creation of [[Homunculus|homunculi]], and has taken over one of the lodge's two towers for use as his [[Laboratory|laboratory]].
