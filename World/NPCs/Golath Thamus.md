> **Golath Thamus**, the so called 'Slip King' of [[Vyre]], is the day-to-day manager of [[Heavenly House|the Heavenly House]] hostel in [[Vise|the Vise]] district of Vyre. Golath Thamus manages the Heavenly House hostel at the behest of its true owner the [[Lunar naga|lunar naga]] [[Pirreta]], who is secretly the [[Kings and Queens of Vyre|Blood Queen of Vyre]] and serves as one of the city's five rulers.
