> The **Unflawed Vault** is a location in [[Nirvana]] associated with the [[Empyreal lord|empyreal lord]] [[Bharnarol]]. The Unflawed Vault lies below Bharnarol's workshop for, while Bharnarol, known as "The Tempered Inventor", goes to great lengths to prevent the release of dangerous new inventions into the [[Great Beyond|multiverse]], he cannot bear to destroy any invention, no matter how vile. Instead, he keeps them locked away within the depths of the Unflawed Vault, which is located beneath the foothills of the Dragonmane Mountains.
