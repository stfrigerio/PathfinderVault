# Wine of the Blood

**Item 5**

> This wine, prepared using the blood of a creature with overwhelming vitality, soothes the body, mind, and spirit with a temporary sense of euphoria and rest. When you drink *wine of the blood* , you recover 2d10 Hit Points. Additionally, the wine attempts to [[Counteract]] every negative [[Emotion]] effect affecting you, with a counteract level of 3 and a counteract modifier of +9. Lastly, you gain a +1 status bonus to all Will saves, or a +2 status bonus against emotion effects, for 1 minute.
