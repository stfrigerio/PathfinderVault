# Iron Medallion

**Item 10**

> This small medallion is shaped like a shield. When you activate it, you gain a +2 status bonus to saves against fear for 1 minute. On the triggering save, if the outcome of your roll is a failure, you get a success instead or if the outcome is a critical failure, you get a failure instead.
