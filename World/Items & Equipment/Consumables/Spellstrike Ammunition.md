The spell must be of a spell level the ammunition can hold, and the spell must be able to target a creature other than the caster. A creature hit by activated *spellstrike ammunition* is targeted by the spell. If the spell requires a saving throw, the target attempts the save against your spell DC.The maximum level of spell the ammunition can hold determines its item level and Price.
