# Book of Translation

**Item 2+**

> The book is leather-bound and decorated with the symbol of the Lantern Lodge. Red and gold cords wrap around the book and tie to keep it shut. Each volume offers translation for a different language. When working with a book of translation, you can attempt [[Diplomacy]] checks to [[Gather Information]] or to [[Make An Impression]] with creatures that speak the language featured in the book, even if you do not speak the language.
