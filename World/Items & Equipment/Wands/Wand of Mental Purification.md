# Wand of Mental Purification

**Item 4+**

> Red feathers hang from the handle of this ivory wand. Holding it brings a sense of gentle calm.
**Activate**: Cast a Spell;
**Frequency**: once per day, plus overcharge;
**Effect**: You cast *[[Soothe]]* of the indicated level, and can attempt to [[Counteract]] one [[Mental]] effect on the same target. Treat the *soothe* spell's level as 1 higher for this counteract check.
**Craft Requirements**: Supply a casting of *soothe* of the appropriate level.
