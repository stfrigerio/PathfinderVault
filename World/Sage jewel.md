 [[Amenopheus]], the Sapphire Sage, holder of the *sapphire sage jewel*.
> The ***sage jewels***, also known as ***sage stones***, are [[Artifact|artifacts]] of [[Ancient Osirion|ancient Osirion]] used to pass down knowledge across millennia. These [[Magic|magical]] [[Gem|gemstones]], which date to at least [[-3000 AR]], have been passed down and rediscovered throughout their history. The [[Jeweled Sages]] are dedicated to locating and reuniting the *sage jewels*.
> Known *sage jewels*, either intact or destroyed, include the amethyst, diamond, emerald, sapphire, topaz, onyx, spinel, and ruby *sage jewels*.
