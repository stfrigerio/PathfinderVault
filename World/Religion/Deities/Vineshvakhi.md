> **Vineshvakhi** is a minor [[Deity|deity]] of guardians and protector of celestial harems and treasure vaults, one of the thousand gods of [[Vudra]].
> According to legend, when the god Vineshvakhi failed to prevent an [[Asura|asura]] raid, he cut off the six fingers of his hand and threw them down to the earth. The [[Calikang|calikangs]] crawled from the devastated craters where these fingers fell.
