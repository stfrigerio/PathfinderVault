> **Inma** is the most powerful [[Oni daimyo|oni daimyo]] dwelling in [[Tian Xia]].


## Appearance

> Inma appears as dark-skinned, three-eyed, four-armed [[Void yai|void yai]].


## Home

> Inma's stronghold, the [[Nightford Shrine]], is a small fortress located in a hidden valley under the highest peak in the [[Nightford Mountains]] in [[Chu Ye]]. Its vast vaults extend deep into the [[Darklands of Tian Xia|Darklands]] below, and the so-called Empress of the World has spent centuries inside it without stepping out into the world she claims to rule.
