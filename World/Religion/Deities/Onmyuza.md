> **Onmyuza** is a particularly hedonistic and debauched [[Ogre mage|ogre mage]] [[Oni daimyo|oni daimyo]] known as the "Dancer in Flesh" whose third eye is stitched shut by three golden rings. She lives underground, in a complex beneath [[Goka]].
> One of the rituals performed by those dedicated to Onmyuza involves dipping a needle in sake and then using it to lightly trace over the lines of the worshippers tattoos.
