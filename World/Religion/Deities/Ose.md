> The [[Infernal duke|infernal duke]] **Ose** holds court in his city of [[Ghaunapthal]], in the [[Outlands]] of [[Dis]]. He commands mighty infernal forges powered by lost [[Soul|souls]], which produce [[Hell]]'s most notorious war machines. Guarded by his 30 diabolical legions, the Duke of Claws is said to be developing a brobdingnagian device powered by an ancient [[Protean|protean]] relic which has the power to drive men [[Diseases of Golarion|insane]] just by gazing at it.
