> **Arqueros** is a powerful [[Archon|archon]] [[Empyreal lord|empyreal lord]]. He typifies the stalwart defender, protecting against corruption and [[Evil|evil]]. When he takes on a charge to protect, he sends multitudes of his minions as aid. He currently holds the title of [[Lord of the Wall]] for [[Threshold Heaven|Threshold]], the lowest level of [[Heaven]].



## Appearance

> The Golden Bulwark is 12 feet tall, wears shining [[Gold|golden]] [[PathfinderWiki/Items/Armors/Armor|armor]], and wields a longspear with [[Diamond|diamond]] blades.


## History

> It is likely that [[Ragathiel|Ragathiels]] ascendancy to the ranks of the empyrean was only made possible, during the Concordance to discuss this topic, because Arqueros took an oath to look after and watch the [[Half-devil|half-devil]] if he became an empyreal lord.


## Church of Arqueros

> Temples devoted to Arqueros are located in cities at war, palaces, and trade cities. His faithful pray every day while holding a [[Heavy shield|heavy shield]] over their heads. His favored minions are celestial [[Aurumvorax|aurumvoraxes]], [[Guardian naga|guardian nagas]], and [[Shield archon|shield archons]].
