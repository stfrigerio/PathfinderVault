> **Atreia, the Lambent King,** is the benevolent [[Elemental lord|elemental lord]] of fire, currently imprisoned in the *Garnet Brand*.



## History

> Atreia began squabbling with fellow [[Good|good]] elemental lords, and was blamed by [[Lysianassa]] for enabling arson, when [[Mortality|mortals]] began using the elements for [[Evil|evil]], while their evil rivals teamed up and imprisoned them.


## Appearance and emissaries

> Atreia resembles a three-headed, six-winged ibis with glowing eyes.


## Church of Atreia


### Worshipers and clergy

> Even though he is blocked from providing [[Divine magic|divine magic]], Atreia is still worshipped by some [[Salamander|salamanders]] for his dominion over protection and life-giving fire, and by mortal cults who exalt the discovery and purification of evil.
