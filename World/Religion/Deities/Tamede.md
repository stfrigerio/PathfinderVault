> **Tamede the Fungal-Hearted** is a [[Harbinger|harbinger]] (a powerful [[Daemon|daemon]] capable of granting [[Divine magic|divine magic]]) who makes her home in the Citadel of Hungry Roots on [[Abaddon]]. She resembles a corpse-like woman with [[Fungi|fungi]] protruding from her exposed flesh, clad in rusty [[PathfinderWiki/Items/Armors/Armor|armor]], and she sits on a pile of skulls. She is a servant of [[Apollyon]], and is served in turn by [[Fungal creature template|fungal]] [[Daemon|daemons]] and [[PathfinderWiki/Geography/Cities/Petitioner|petitioners]].
