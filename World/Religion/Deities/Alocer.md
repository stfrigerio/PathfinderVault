> **Alocer** is an [[Infernal duke|infernal duke]], also known as the "Pack Lord". The most obedient hunter of [[Hell]], he spent centuries in the forges of [[Malebolge]], and is venerated by both hunters and those upon whom they prey. He appears as a fire-breathing archer with the head of a [[Lion|lion]] and the talons of an [[PathfinderWiki/Geography/Cities/Eagle|eagle]], wearing heavy [[PathfinderWiki/Items/Armors/Armor|armor]].
