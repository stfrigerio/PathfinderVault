> **Soralyon** is an [[Angel|angel]] [[Empyreal lord|empyreal lord]] who watches over sacred monuments and keeps [[Magic|magic]] secrets, which he shares with a deserving few.



## Home

> Soralyon watches over the [[Maelstrom]] from his post at the Steward's Stone in [[Nirvana]].


## Appearance

> Soralyon appears as a man carved of the purest black marble, and has also been depicted in [[Art|art]] in numerous different colours. His form always glimmers softly from within.


## Relationships

> Due to his willingness to intercede when [[Mortality|mortals]] seek to uncover (even unwillingly) dangerous monuments or ancient lore, Soralyon is sometimes at odds with preservationists like [[Bharnarol]] or caretakers of the [[Great Library of Harmonious Scripture]].


## Cults

> Guardians of ancient sites, particularly self-appointed ones, usually pray to Soralyon for strength and perseverance so they may continue their vigil. He is also worshipped by prudent, [[Good|good]]-aligned researchers.


### Holy symbol

> Soralyon's [[Holy symbol|holy symbol]] is a crooked spire of stone covered in strange, [[Astronomy|astronomical]] writings.


## On Golarion

> Soralyon is one of the empyreal lords worshiped in the [[Varisia|Varisian]] city of [[Magnimar]], particularly by the [[Runeguard|runeguards]], who seek to reclaim the knowledge of the [[Seven virtues of rule|seven virtues of rule]] that once powered [[Thassilon|Thassilonian]] rune magic before it was corrupted by the [[Runelords|runelords]].
