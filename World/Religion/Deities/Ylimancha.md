> **Ylimancha** is an [[Agathion|agathion]] [[Empyreal lord|empyreal lord]] who watches over coastlines and protects those whose livelihood depends on the sea. She is said to have taught fisherfolk how to build breakwaters to protect ports from the sea, how to find schools of [[Fish|fish]], and cautioned them against overfishing.



## Home

> Ylimancha lives in a great [[Gold|golden]] aerie on the Skyward Cliffs, on the shore of the [[Sea of No Shadows]] in [[Nirvana]].


## Appearance

> Ylimancha has two forms: one of an [[Osprey|osprey]]-headed woman carrying a teak longbow strung with gold; and another of a [[Siren|siren]] with the body of a white seagull and the head of a [[Varisian]] crone with short dark hair. In both forms, she speaks with a voice carrying the echo of crashing waves.


## Relationships

> Since the dawn of time, Ylimancha has been fighting against [[Pazuzu]] over the [[Soul|souls]] of those that fly. She bitterly resents those races lost to his [[Evil|evil]] influence.


## Servants

> Ylimancha is served by many [[Chelonidal|chelonidals]], who are often front-line infantry in agathion armies.


## Cults


### Holy symbol

> Ylimancha's [[Holy symbol|holy symbol]] is a golden gull with three concentric blue rings on each outstretched wing.


## On Golarion

> Ylimancha's cult is popular in the [[Varisia|Varisian]] city of [[Magnimar]].
