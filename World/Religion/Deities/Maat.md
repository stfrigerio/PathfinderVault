> **Maat** (or **Ma'at** as it is sometimes spelled) is the [[Osirian pantheon|Ancient Osirian goddess]] of balance, justice, and truth, guardian of cosmic [[Law|order]]. She embodies the concepts that a [[Pharaoh|pharaoh]] must uphold for a legitimate, efficient reign, lest everything dissolve into [[Chaos|chaos]]. She is also responsible for the movement of [[Celestial body|celestial bodies]] across the sky, and the procession of the seasons.



## Appearance

> Maat appears as a woman with a blue [[Ostrich|ostrich]] feather on her head and winged arms, carrying an ankh and a was scepter representing life and power.


## Relationships

![[Osirian_Pantheon.jpg|right+hmed]] 
 A Family Tree of the Osirian Pantheon.
> Maat is the daughter of [[Ra]], sister of [[Bastet]], [[Hathor]] and [[Sekhmet]], and wife of [[Thoth]]. She presides over the councils of the [[Deity|gods]] due to her impartiality and honesty, assists [[Pharasma]] in the judgement of [[Soul|souls]], and opposes [[Set]] and [[Apep]] for their attempts to sow [[Chaos|chaos]].


## Cults

> Maat is worshipped by [[Paladin|paladins]] as the embodiment of justice, and [[Monk|monks]] and [[Oracle|oracles]] for the cosmic truth she embodies.
