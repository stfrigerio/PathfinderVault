> **Shiggarreb** is a huge [[Qlippoth lord|qlippoth lord]] resembling a [[Drider|drider]] with multiple arms and a split, deformed face. She serves as the messenger for qlippoth lords and often leads [[Qlippoth|qlippoth]] invasions of [[Material Plane]] [[Planet|planets]] and [[Abyssal realm|Abyssal realms]] held by [[Demon|demons]]. The ground that she walks on becomes twisted and warped by the influence of the deep [[Abyss]].


## On Golarion

> On [[Golarion]], Shiggarreb is worshipped by the enlightened [[Xulgath|xulgaths]] dwelling under the central ziggurat of [[Deep Tolguth]]; she also counts among her followers a minority of degenerate [[Troglodyte|troglodytes]], most of whom have converted to the worship of [[Zevgavizeb]], a former qlippoth lord turned [[Demon lord|demon lord]]. She bestows forbidden [[Magic|magic]] on her xulgath followers, who believe that the false sun of Deep Tolguth will one day become a [[Portal|portal]] to her realm.
