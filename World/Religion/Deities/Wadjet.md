> The [[Serpent|serpent]] cult of the benevolent **Wadjet** is a twin to the cult of the deceiver [[Apep]]. Wadjet, a great river serpent said to dwell in the papyrus marshes of the [[River Sphinx]] delta, is a tutelary [[Deity|deity]] and mother of her emissaries, the [[Uraeus|uraeuses]]. Wadjet is the nurturer of the River Sphinx and a personification of the spirits guiding and guarding the ruler of [[Osirion]]. The *[[Rod of the uraeus|rod of the uraeus]]* is a [[Magic item|magic item]] fashioned in the form of a uraeus-headed [[Staff|staff]], which assists in the detection of deceit and can create a spray of stinging [[Poison|poison]].
