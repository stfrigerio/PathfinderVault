> **Bergelmir**, also known as the **Mother of Memories**, is a venerable [[Storm giant|storm giant]] [[Demigod|demigoddess]] of family, elders, and genealogy, who keeps track of their family lineages in an enormous tome. She represents the storm giant race's strong connection to home and family. She is said to have a familial connection to [[Skrymir]], the trickster god of the storm giants.
