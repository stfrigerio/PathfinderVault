> **Yaezhing** is the [[Tian-Shu]] deity of murder, death, and punishment which exceeds the crime; he is patron of [[Ninja|ninja]] and [[PathfinderWiki/Classes/Assassin|assassins]]. He also acts as the enforcer of justice among the gods. His unholy symbol is a shuriken drenched in blood.

## Appearance

> Yaezhing is portrayed in idols as a Tian male in red robes wearing an iron crown holding several shuriken in one hand and a noose in the other. Physically, he appears with bulging eyes, a long beard and a red face.
