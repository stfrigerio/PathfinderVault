> **Tlehar, Sun Goddess of Dawn** and **Lioness Goddess** of [[Iron|iron]], love, and rebirth, is one of the [[Old Sun Gods]] of [[Mzali]].



## Relationships

> As one of the Old Sun Gods, Tlehar is opposed to [[Walkena]].


## Appearance and emissaries

> Tlehar is most often depicted as a [[Human|human]] with the head of a gray-furred, black-eyed [[Lion|lioness]].


### Worshipers and clergy

> As one of the Old Sun Gods, Tlehar is worshipped by the Bright Lions, a group of anti-Walkena revolutionaries.
