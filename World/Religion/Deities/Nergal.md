*This article is on the [[Infernal duke|infernal duke]]. For the [[Demon lord|demon lord]], see [[Nurgal]].*
> **Nergal** is a merciless [[Infernal duke|infernal duke]] associated with wartime atrocities, riding to battle with [[Poison|poison]], [[Plague|plague]], and fire to bedevil all soldiers.


## History

> Nergal was created from the lesser half of the [[Deity|deity]] [[Nurgal]], which plunged into [[Hell]] after [[Acavna]] clove him in two and sent both parts tumbling into the [[Maelstrom]]. The greater half, which fell into the [[Abyss]], kept the name Nurgal and became a [[Demon lord|demon lord]], for whom Nergal maintains a seething jealousy.
