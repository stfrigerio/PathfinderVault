> **Losarkur** is an [[Infernal duke|infernal duke]] in the service of the [[Archdevil|archdevil]] [[Barbatos]]. He lives on the first layer of [[Hell]], where he breeds packs of vicious [[Hell hound|hell hounds]] and [[Cerberi|cerberi]] in vast kennel-caverns. A legion of [[Edavagor|edavagors]] serves him. For his loyalty, Losarkur is deemed one of Barbatos's most trusted servants.
