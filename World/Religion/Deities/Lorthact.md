> **Lorthact** is a powerful [[Infernal duke|infernal duke]] who has spent the last several hundred years hiding from the wrath of the [[Eiseth|Erinyes Queen]].

## Home

> Lorthact lives in the [[Varisia|Varisian]] city-state of [[Korvosa]] where he has been growing his power and influence for centuries.


## Appearance

> Lorthact is a medium-sized humanoid devil with five horns protruding from his forehead that bears a resemblance to a crown. He has bronze toned skin, black hair, cloven feet, and clawed hands. His sclera is a pale yellow with no visible iris or pupil.


## Relationships

> Lorthact served as an advisor on magical subjects for the [[Archdevils|archdevils]] [[Geryon]] and [[Mephistopheles]]. He was once a consort of [[Eiseth]], one of the [[Queens of the Night]].
