> **Westport** is the larger of [[Oppara]]'s two ports. It services all international traffic and [[Taldan Imperial Navy]] supply traffic. Westport district is full of inns, bars, brothels, gambling houses, drug dens, and moneylenders. The docks themselves are well guarded and lit at night. [[Hallador|Westport Harbormaster Hallador]] formed the [[Porthmos Militia]] to keep watch on the capital's largest port at night.
