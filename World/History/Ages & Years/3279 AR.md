## 3279 AR in conflicts

>  - The [[dwarven]] hero [[Khadon the Mighty]] finally defeats the [[Orc]] inhabitants of the [[Five Kings Mountains]] at the [[Battle of Splitmist Pass]] after [[82 years]] of conflict.
