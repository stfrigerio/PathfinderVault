> **Typhon** is a deceased [[Archdevil|archdevil]], the former ruler of [[Avernus]], the first layer of [[Hell]]. Ruling from the garden domain of [[Arima]], he was slain by the [[Empyreal lord|empyreal lord]] [[Ragathiel]], possibly at the urging of [[Asmodeus]] as punishment for a dalliance with the [[Demon lord|demon lord]] [[Lamashtu]]. Typhon was succeeded as Lord of the First by [[Barbatos]].
> Typhon presented the [[Infernal duke|infernal duke]] [[Furcas]] with a [[Trident|trident]] called the *[[Avernus Claw]]* that now serves as the infernal duke's [[Unholy symbol|unholy symbol]].


## Appearance

> Typhon had dragon-like wings, a serpentine tail, and poisonous claws.
