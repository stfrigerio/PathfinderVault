> **Lady Seleenae of House Damaq** is a member of the [[Absalom|Absalomian]] nobility and the [[Nomarch|nomarch]] of Western Council, the [[Government of AbsalomDistrict councils of Absalom|district council]] of the [[Westgate|Westgate district]]. She also holds the title of Head Tax Collector of the [[Sally Gate]], and is the owner of the Foreign Coin Exchange.
