> **Whitemarch Prefecture** is the south-easternmost of the 14 modern political divisions of the nation of [[Taldor]]. A mountainous region, the [[Prefecture|prefecture]] has traded hands with [[Qadira]] many times over the past few thousand years.


## Government

> The ruler of Whitemarch Prefecture is the Grand Duchess Vivexis Darahan.


## People

> Whitemarch is also home to House Trant, a belligerent noble family that uses the threat of future Qadiran aggression to maintain control of their holdings. The elder Trant serves the Taldan government as a senator.
