*See also: [[Verduran Forest]]*
> The **Verduran Forest** is one of the five general regions of the nation of [[Andoran]], the others being the capital district of [[Almas region|Almas]], coastal [[Arthfell region|Arthfell]], the [[Carpenden Plains region|Carpenden Plains]], and [[Darkmoon Vale]]. The Verduran Forest region is named after and is situated in the portion of the great [[Verduran Forest]] that is located within the borders of Andoran, and includes all forested land west of the [[Sellen River]].
