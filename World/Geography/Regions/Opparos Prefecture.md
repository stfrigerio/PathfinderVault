> **Opparos Prefecture** is the capital [[Prefecture|prefecture]] of the nation of [[Taldor]], and contains the city of [[Oppara]]. It is one of the country's 14 political divisions, and is located on the coast of the [[Inner Sea]] between [[Tandak Prefecture|Tandak]] and [[Moda Prefecture|Moda]] [[Prefecture|Prefectures]].
