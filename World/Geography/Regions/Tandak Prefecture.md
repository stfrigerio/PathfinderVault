> **Tandak Prefecture** is one of the 14 modern political divisions of the nation of [[Taldor]]. Named after the [[Tandak Plains]], the [[Prefecture|prefecture]] begins at the border with [[Andoran]] east of the [[Sellen River]] and south of the [[Verduran Forest]]. It contains the major city of [[Cassomir]] as well as the smaller settlement of [[Ridonport]].
