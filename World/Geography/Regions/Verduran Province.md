> **Verduran Province** is one of the 14 major political divisions of the nation of [[Taldor]], and encompasses the vast [[Verduran Forest|woodland]] located within that country. Unlike the settled and well-governed [[Prefecture|prefectures]] of Taldor, the Verduran is largely a wilderness with occasional pockets of [[Human|human]] settlement.
