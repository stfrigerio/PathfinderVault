> **Moda Prefecture** is one of the 14 political divisions of the nation of [[Taldor]], and its southwesternmost [[Prefecture|prefecture]]. Located on the [[Inner Sea]], Moda Prefecture contains the [[Western Rushing Marches]], the port city of [[Golsifar]] and the towns of [[Merciful Bay]] and [[Railford]]. The region's ruler is Grand Duke Nestor Delriddia.
