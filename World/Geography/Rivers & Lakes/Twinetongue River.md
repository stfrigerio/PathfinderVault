> The **Twinetongue River** is a small river which runs for about 35 miles, in a south-south-easterly manner, in the eastern part of the county of [[Varno]] in [[Ustalav]]. The river flows into the [[Sellen River|West Sellen River]] in the south-east corner of Varno on the border with the [[River Kingdoms]].
