> The Blue Bunyip is a tavern in the city of [[Magnimar]]'s [[Naos]] district. It is a front for the group the [[Midnight Dawn]], so they can watch the nobles and guards of the city without exposing themselves. It is also the place you need to be vetted if you want to buy the new [[Recreational drugs of Golarion|drug]] [[Midnight milk|midnight milk]].
