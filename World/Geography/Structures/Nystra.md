**Nystra** is a ghost-town in the far-eastern reaches of the [[River Kingdoms]].  Once a thriving community centered around the cultivation and production of the fine [[Silk|silk]] produced by the insectoid [[Silk gargoyle|silk gargoyles]], or [[Silkgoyle|silkgoyles]], its inhabitants were totally wiped out in a revenge plot by the [[Taldan human ethnicity|Taldan]] [[Rules/Classes/Druid|druid]] Tharl Grimull in [[4664 AR]].
