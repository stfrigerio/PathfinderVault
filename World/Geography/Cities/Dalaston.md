> **Dalaston** is a large town on the southwestern edge of [[Taldor]]'s [[World's Edge Mountains|Worlds Edge Mountains]]. Although members of all the major races reside in the town, [[Dwarf|dwarves]] are the second-largest group after [[Human|humans]]. The three major employers are the local [[Iron|iron]] mine (owned by the Dalassenos family), the Morian Foundry, and the Irini brewery.
