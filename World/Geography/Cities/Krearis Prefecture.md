> **Krearis Prefecture** is one of the 14 political divisions of the nation of [[Taldor]], and the inland [[Prefecture|prefecture]] with the highest population. It lies in northern Taldor between the [[Falling River]] in the north to the [[World's Edge Mountains|Worlds Edge Mountains]] in the south and the [[Verduran Forest]] in the west.
