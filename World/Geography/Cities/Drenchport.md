> The city of **Drenchport** is on [[Tempest Cay]] in the [[Shackles]]. Its proximity to the dreaded [[Eye of Abendego]] means the city is constantly lashed by storms. The city itself is constructed from driftwood and the remains of wrecked [[Ship|ships]].
> Drenchport's most infamous tavern is the [[Drowned Dwarf]]. It serves surprisingly tasty [[Alcoholic beverages|black kelp beer]], but is notorious for its very high murder rate.
> The ruler of Drenchport is the [[Pirate lord|pirate lord]] known as the [[Master of the Gales]].
> The city is the home of the [[Gnome|gnome]] salvager Tho Rimplethember, who is well known as an expert on the currents and winds of the dangerous Eye of Abendego.
