> **Maashinelle** is the largest settlement in [[Lambreth]] and serves as the seat of power for its dictator, [[Kamdyn Arnefax|Lord Kamdyn Arnefax]]. The town lies in the lowlands near the [[Sellen River]] and has a large dockside along the water. Farms, ranches, vineyards, [[Animal|animal]] trainers, inns, and blacksmiths can all be found in Maashinelle, but the town's most famous export is soft wool known as "Cullerton wool."
