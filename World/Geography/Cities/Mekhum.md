> The fishing village of **Mekhum** is one of a handful of [[Rahadoum|Rahadoumi]] settlements in the arid lands south of the [[Napsune Mountains]]. The village's residents tithe a "[[Malsandra|drowned ghost]]" who inhabits the [[Oath Wars]]-ruined [[Mekhum Temple|temple]] of a long-forgotten god in the marshy outskirts of town.
