> **Fort Fang** is the headquarters of its region's [[World/Geography/Mountains & Hills/Gnoll|gnolls]], and has been for decades. It houses around 500 soldiers and slavers and 1,500 [[Slavery|slaves]] at any given time.
> The fort is the target of frequent attacks by the [[Eagle Knights]], as part of their abolitionist activities.
