> **Ul-Angorn** is a small town on the [[Crown of the World]], lying on the shores of [[Saltsea Lake]]. It is the largest settlement of [[Erutaki human ethnicity|Erutaki]] [[Human|humans]] in the Ruun Uvas basin. Ul-Angorn is a resting spot for caravans along the [[Path of Aganhei]] between the [[High Ice]] and [[Ovorikheer Pass]].
> Ul-Angorn is prosperous for a town its size, and its inhabitants are inquisitive and garrulous.
