> The town of **Eldentre** can be found in the south-western corner of the Linnorm Kingdom of [[Hagreach]] in the [[Lands of the Linnorm Kings]], on the banks of the [[Thundering River]]. The town is famous for the Lightning Oak, and the weapons that are made using the electrical energy from this tree.
