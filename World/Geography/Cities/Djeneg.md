> **Djeneg** is a village in the [[Footprints of Rovagug]] area of [[Osirion]]. It is located near the mineral spring known as [[Great Pool|The Great Pool]]. The Great Pool has been visited by Osirian nobles for millennia, and the village of Djeneg grew up around the pool to support its visitors.
