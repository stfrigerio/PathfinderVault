> **High Ninshabur** is a [[Holy|holy]] city of [[Sarenrae]] on the coast of the [[Sea of No Shadows]] in the [[Plane|plane]] of [[Nirvana]], the Dawnflower's [[Outer Sphere]] home. It is one of the largest cities in Nirvana, and unlike the rest of Everlight, Sarenrae's realm is open to followers of other faiths.


## People

> The population of High Ninshabur consists of [[Mortal|mortal]] worshipers of [[Sarenrae]], including descendants of those from the ancient nation of [[Ninshabur]] destroyed by the [[Tarrasque]].


## Economy

> High Ninshabur is famed for selling divinely inspired tools of [[Magic|magical]] [[Healing|healing]] crafted by Sarenrae's clergy.
