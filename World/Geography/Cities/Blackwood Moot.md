> **Blackwood Moot** is an isolated stand of [[Blackwood wood|Blackwood trees]] deep in the [[Verduran Forest]] of [[Taldor]] that serves as a meeting place for wild residents dissatisfied with the [[Treaty of the Wildwood]]. Those who meet at Blackwood Moot view the members of the [[Wildwood Lodge]] as traitors who have enabled Taldor's destruction of the Verduran Forest.
> The Blackwood Moot is led by the [[Arboreal regent|arboreal regent]] [[Oakadence]], a powerful [[Sorcerer|sorcerer]] who has been organising secretive raids on [[Wispil]] and the [[Isle of Arenway]] using the wild beasts of the forest as disposable foot soldiers.
