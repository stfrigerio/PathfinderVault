> **Anchor's End** is a [[Cheliax|Chelish]] colony in [[Arcadia]]. [[Ship|Ships]] set sail from [[Kintargo]] to make the voyage to the distant colony. Anchor's End provides [[Gold|gold]] and [[Slave|slaves]] to the Chelish empire.
> The settlement also exports red and black Arcadian marble back to Cheliax. This marble is favoured by [[Egorian|Egorians]] noble class and the government, who have begun incorporating the marble in their dwellings and civic buildings.
> The few surviving residents of its sister colony to the north, [[Canorus]], moved to Anchor's End after an invasion from [[Dark folk|dark folk]] and a [[Telgrodradt|telgrodradt]].
