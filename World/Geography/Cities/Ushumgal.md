> **Ushumgal** is a wealthy city on the [[Alavah Peninsula]] of [[Qadira]].


## Geography

> Ushumgal exudes wealth with its architecture of tall towers and buildings of stone filigree.


## Government

> The city is ruled by the Sisters of Bronze, a group of women rumored to be [[Bronze dragon|bronze dragons]] in [[Human|human]] form. Despite its wealth, Ushumgal has few guards but maintains a reputation of safety, with most people entering only on invitation.
