> **Chitterhome** is the largest [[Ratfolk|ratfolk]] settlement in [[Numeria]]. It is located in the [[Sellen Hills]] region, about 50 miles east of [[Scrapwall]]. The settlement itself is a square mile in size and consists of natural caverns that have been further excavated.
> Chitterhome contains three, and possibly more, carefully guarded entrances to [[Nar-Voth]].
> Outsiders are not allowed in Chitterhome, but its residents trade throughout eastern Numeria. Specifically, Chitterhome traders travel to [[Chesed]] and [[Hajoth Hakados]] and along the [[Sellen River]].
