> The **Hold of Elistia**, or **Fortress of the Bluffs**, is a military outpost of the [[Elf|elven]] kingdom of [[Kyonin]], located in the north-eastern part of the [[Dwarf|dwarven]] nation of the [[Five Kings Mountains]]. Elistia has a population of 500 elves ruled by [[Alavar|Lord Alavar]].
> The Hold is protected by the **Sentinels of Elistia**, led by Lord Alavar in his role as **Captain of the Sentinels**.
