> **Blackblood Cay** is a small port town on [[Shark Island]] in the [[Shackles]]. It is located on a small hidden inlet of the island and was founded by [[Simeon the Vain|Free Captain Simeon the Vain]] in [[4703 AR]]. The port now hosts ten more [[Pirate|pirate]] captains, while Simeon remains lord of Blackblood Cay. The town features berths along the wharf for only six vessels at a time.


## Inhabitants

> Simeon's [[Ship|sloop]] *[[Blushing Bride]]* is known for its extraordinary speed. Rumor has it that Simeon intends to contest [[Avimar Sorrinash|Avimar Sorrinashs]] dominance of Shark Island.
> Other notable residents of the town include [[Arkel Kruggut]], the [[Kellid]] bartender of the seedy tavern [[Fallen from Grace]].
