> **Fort Bandu** is a fortification in eastern [[Sargava]], located in the [[Bandu Hills]] on the banks of the [[River of Lost Tears]] near the border with the [[Mwangi Expanse]].



## History

> Fort Bandu was established in [[4164 AR]] to protect the mining operations in the nearby [[Bandu Hills]] (particularly [[Farsouth Mine]]) and has suffered repeated attacks from the native [[Mwangi human ethnicity|Mwangi]] tribes, especially the [[Bandu]].


## Leadership

> The commander of the fort and its 150-strong garrison of Sargavan Guard soldiers is the aging, but still capable [[Sylien|Praetor Sylien]]. Nearly all of the soldiers are used in the defense of the fort and guarding its supply route.


## Current projects

> The [[Rivermen's Guild|Rivermens Guild]] transports goods to and from Fort Bandu all the way to [[Port Freedom]]. Workers from the fort are attempting to build a bridge across the River of Lost Tears, but are being by hampered by the Bandu.
