> **Corpselight** is a small farming settlement in the undead nation of [[Geb]] situated north of the [[Axan Wood]] and about two day's travel east of [[Graydirge]].
> Corpselight is not a traditional farming community however. The town raises intelligent humanoids for use as food and sustenance for the various undead beings throughout [[Geb]]. These human chattel are kept in pens until they are ready to be shipped out. Standard crops are also grown in order to feed them.
