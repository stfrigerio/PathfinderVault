> **Crossfen** is a [[Nirmathas|Nirmathi]] village in the [[Fangwood]] near the [[World/Geography/Nations/Lastwall]] border and a mile-wide wetland. Of the settlements inside the Fangwood, Crossfen is one of the few capable of supporting cows that feed on thick-growing sedge.
> The wetlands host [[Boggard|boggards]] and [[Will-o'-wisp|will-o-wisps]] known to claim visitors and villagers who attempt to cross it at night.
