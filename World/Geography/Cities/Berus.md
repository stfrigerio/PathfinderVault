> **Berus** is a very prosperous settlement along the northern border of the [[Ustalav|Ustalavic]] county of [[Ardeal]] on the banks of the [[River of Scales]].


## Religion

> The people of the town of Berus give thanks to Mother Sighle for the amazing abundance of the town and their prosperity. Mother Sighle is apparently a local goddess newly 'rediscovered' by the populace. Since the rediscovery, the town has become noticeably less friendly to outsiders, however.
