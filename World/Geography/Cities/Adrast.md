> The **Barony of Adrast** is a small barony and settlement in the north-west of [[Taldor]], located north of the [[Verduran Forest]] and south of the westernmost mountains of the [[Fog Peaks]] in the [[Northern Tandak Prefecture]]. The Baron of Adrast is [[Stelan|Baron Stelan]] who is married to [[Lenelle|Baroness Lenelle]]. The headsman of **Adrast** settlement, a small village, is [[Odric]]; while the captain of the guard is the [[Half-orc|half-orc]] [[Drelm|Captain Drelm]]. Following the professed faith of the baron, the religion predominantly followed is that of [[Abadar]]; the barony has a [[Cleric|cleric]] and temple to that [[Deity|god]].
