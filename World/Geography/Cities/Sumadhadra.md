> **Sumadhadra** is a massive port city that sits on the easternmost tip of the [[Divine Garden]] region where it meets the [[Embaral Ocean]] and it is the most eastern city in all [[Vudra]]. Sumadhadra is a prominent centre of trade; in particular, it has strong links with the continent of [[Tian Xia]] and it is where most visitors from that continent first enter Vudra. Sumadhadra is the home city of the [[Monk|monk]] [[Sajan Gadadvara|Sajan]].
