> **Rosehaven** is a small [[Galt|Galtan]] village located on the [[East Sellen River]] south of the [[River Kingdoms]] city of [[Liberthane]]. They celebrate an annual three-day autumn festival known as [[Mercylight]], commemorating the community's defeat of a small coven of [[Gyronna|Gyronna-worshipers]] several decades ago.
