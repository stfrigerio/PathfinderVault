> **Babschu-Jong** is a major city in the [[Samsaran|samsaran]] country of [[Zi Ha]] on the continent of [[Tian Xia]]. It lies on a southern peak in the [[Zi Ha Mountains|Zi Ha mountain range]] and is a major trade connection on the route between the [[Amanandar|Amanandarans]]' capital of [[New Oppara]] and Zi Ha's nominal capital of [[Sangpo-Jong]]. Primary trade materials include [[Art|artwork]], [[Gem|gems]], herbs, incense, livestock, and stone from Zi Ha, which are traded for grain, [[Iron|iron]], and textiles.
