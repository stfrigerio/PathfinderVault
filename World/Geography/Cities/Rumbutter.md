> **Rumbutter** is the southernmost settlement on [[Bag Island]] in [[Shackles]]. It is a sprawling, hilly, farming community known for its apiaries and [[Honey|honey]]. [[Rumbutter honey]] finds its way throughout the Shackles and [[Sargava]]. Rumbutter's three taverns on its main road, [[Bedda's Berth|Beddas Berth]], the [[Gilded Lily]], and [[Shopin's Roundhouse|Shopins Roundhouse]], serve unique blends of iced butterscotch [[Beer|beers]] in the rainy season and [[Honey wine|honey wine]] in the drier months.
