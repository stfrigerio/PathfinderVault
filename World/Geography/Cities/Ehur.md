> **Ehur** is a city in [[Qadira]]. Lying in the center of the [[Ketz Desert]], this [[Genie|genie]] city is where denizens of the various [[Elemental Plane|Elemental Planes]] have their embassies and serves as a trading hub for these people within Qadira.


## People

> Along with genies from the Elemental Planes, many [[Geniekin|geniekin]] end up living here in order to escape prejudice and mistreatment faced by them in other parts of Qadira. They believe that serving the genies will give them a better life, though that is not always the case.
