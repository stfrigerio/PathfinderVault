> **New Oppara** is the capital of the [[Taldor|Taldan]] colony [[Amanandar]] on the continent of [[Tian Xia]]. It was rechristened in [[4608 AR|7108 IC]] (4608 AR) by the country's founder, General Orphyrea Amanandar, who defeated the local warlords who had previously controlled it. Its original [[Tien]] name was **Kamikobu**.
