> **Lelag-Leng** is a crude village, built from stone and wood, located in southeastern [[Leng]]. It is home to short, bald, brown-skinned, narrow-eyed [[Humanoid|humanoids]]; while they resemble degenerate [[Human|humans]], their exact race has not been confirmed. They trade exotic [[Silk|silks]] with the [[Denizen of Leng|denizens of Leng]], are ruled and protected by a powerful [[Sorcerer|sorcerer]], and perform profane rites of worship to their [[Elder Mythos|frightening gods]].
