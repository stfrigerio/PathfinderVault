> **Farhaven** is a town in the [[Protectorate of the Black Marquis]]. It has a city guard of approximately 20 guards, and its [[Cattle|cattle]] pens and farms are protected. It features also some legitimate merchant traffic with other [[River Kingdoms]]. Farhaven is still a [[Pirate|pirate]] town, so it has a black market, bar fights, and docks full of scoundrels.
