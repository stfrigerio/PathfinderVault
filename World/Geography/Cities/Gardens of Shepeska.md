> The **Gardens of Shepeska** lie on the north eastern tip of [[Osirion]], where the [[Inner Sea]] meets the [[Obari Ocean]]. They are tended by druids who have granted many saplings to the crown prince at [[Sothis]].


## History

> In [[4212 AR]], a desert [[Rules/Classes/Druid|druid]] named [[Qali Manar]] embarked upon a pilgrimage to the edge of the [[Scorpion Coast]] where he stopped and established a circle of druids that, to this day, still tend the Gardens of Shepeska.
