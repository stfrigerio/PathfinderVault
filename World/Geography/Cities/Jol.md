> The city of **Jol** (pronounced JOL) is located to the south of the [[Grungir Forest]] and is the capital of [[Southmoor]] in the [[Lands of the Linnorm Kings]]. The city is old and built on the ruins of the [[Thassilon|Thassilonian]] city of [[Torandey]]; it was founded in [[1102 AR]] by [[Eskir Honeytongue|King Eskir Honeytongue]]. It is ruled by [[Linnorm King]] [[Opir Eightfingers]], although his pronouncements are often ignored, due to the questions that surround his rise to power.
