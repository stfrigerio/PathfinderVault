> **Dunhob** is a small village in the southern [[Ustalav|Ustalavic]] county of [[Versex]], near [[Lantern Lake]] and the [[Hungry Mountains]]. The village is notable for strange occurrences that started around [[4672 AR]] with the deaths of [[The Lucky Lantern|local tavern]] owner [[Veny Srevka]] and noblewoman [[Rena Illirigarde|Lady Rena Illirigarde]]. The superstitious village is led by town elder Maricella Weath.


## Locations

> - [[The Lucky Lantern]], an abandoned and reportedly haunted tavern once owned by Veny Srevka.
> - An ancient [[Kellid]] monolith about an hour's hike from town.
> - [[Illirigarde Manor]], the abandoned home of Lady Illirigarde, located a few miles from Dunhob in the hills near Lantern Lake.
