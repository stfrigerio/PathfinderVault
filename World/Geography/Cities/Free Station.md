> **Free Station** (sometimes spelled "**Freestation**") is a town built on the confluence of the [[Little Vanji River|Little Vanji]] and [[Vanji River]] in the [[Mwangi Expanse]].
> To the west of the city lies the [[Gutblood Labyrinth]]: a mighty fissure in the [[Rechiend's Plains|Rechiends Plains]], which the locals of the city call [[Calistria|Calistrias]] Grin and which contains [[Ghol-Gan|Ghol-Gani]] ruins.
