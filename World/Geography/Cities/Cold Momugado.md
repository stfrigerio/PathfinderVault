> **Cold Momugado** is the largest of the [[Skum|skum]] settlements on [[Lake Nirthran]]. It is an ancient [[Alghollthu|alghollthu]] ruin near the lake's centre. It is said that beneath the city is the entrance to a distinct mini-realm of the [[Darklands]].
> The skum of Cold Momugado worship a [[Deity|deity]] called [[Shumbauth]], believed to live the depths of the western part of the lake.
