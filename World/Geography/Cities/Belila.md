> **Belila** is a small [[Irrisen|Irriseni]] town of the [[Iceflow]] in the northwestern corner of the province of [[Bleakmarch]]. Much of the town recently converted to new faith known as the [[Way of Transcendent Bliss]], much to the consternation of its ruler, the [[Frederyka Elvanna|Baroness Frederyka]].
