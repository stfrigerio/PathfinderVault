> **Petrograd** was the Imperial capital of [[Russia]], and lies on the Neva River *in the north-western region* of that vast nation on the distant [[Planet|planet]] of [[Earth]]. *Today, it is a major city in the [[Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic]].*



## History

> The city was originally called **Saint Petersburg**, *until [[4709 AR|AD 1914]] when it was changed to Petrograd to remove any German language from the the name of the city; in 1914, Russia had gone to war with the German Empire.*
> The city was the scene of both the February Revolution in [[Pharast|March]], [[4712 AR|AD 1917]] and the October Revolution in [[Neth|November]], AD 1917. *In early [[4713 AR|AD 1918]], the capital of Russia moved to Moscow.*
