> **Vinpinna** is a town sat in the foothills of the [[Pardaparbat Mountains]] in the [[Vudra|Impossible Kingdoms of Vudra]]. Located in the south-western reaches of the [[Narhari Desert]] region, Vinpinna, along with the nearby town of [[Adhaarm]], is controlled by an order of [[Undead|undead]] martial artists.
