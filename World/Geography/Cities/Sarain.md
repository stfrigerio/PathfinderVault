> **Sarain** is a small village in the [[River Kingdoms|River Kingdom]] of [[Pitax]], close to the border with [[Mivon]]. Sarain is famous for its [[Alcoholic beverages|vineyards]] and, before the arrival of [[Castruccio Irovetti]], the Liacenza family had overall control over most of the wine-making business of the area.
