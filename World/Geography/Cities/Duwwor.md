> **Duwwor** is the fourth-largest of [[Thuvia|Thuvias]] five allied city-states. It is situated on the edge of the [[Barrier Wall]] mountains, near the headwaters of the [[Turvanar River]]. Its ruler is [[Kharane]], Defender of Duwwor.
> The people of Duwwor try to live in harmony with the desert, and the city is an ideal place to find a desert guide. A circle of desert [[Rules/Classes/Druid|druids]] live close to the city, and are known to make good allies and bad enemies.
> Duwwor has a large temple to [[Gozreh]].
> As one of Thuvia's five city-states, once every five years it is the city's turn to sell six vials of the prized *[[Sun orchid elixir|sun orchid elixir]]* to foreign bidders.
