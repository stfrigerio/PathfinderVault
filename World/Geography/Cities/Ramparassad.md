> **Ramparassad** is the traditional capital of [[Dtang Ma]], home of its ruling kamraten and their *Cinnamon Throne*, and is the [[Tian Xia]] nation's largest city.


## Geography

> Ramparassad is inland, located at the foot of the active [[Volcano|volcano]] Mount Pho-Yim in the eastern [[Kullan Dei]] mountains and near the border with [[Xa Hoi]].


## Government

> Ramparassad is home to Dtang Ma's ruling council and its supreme leader, the kamraten. Ramparassad, and all of Dtang Ma, are dependent on a strict adherence to the unwritten, informal constitution of the land which fosters harmony by creating a mutual dependence between the various lords of the city and the wider lands.
