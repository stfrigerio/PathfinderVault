> The city of **Trollheim** can be found in [[Hagreach]], the easternmost of the [[Linnorm Kingdoms]], hard against its border with [[Irrisen]] and on the banks of the [[Rimeflow River]]. Trollheim has no current king and is ruled by [[Freyr Darkwine|Castellan Freyr Darkwine]].


## City districts

> The following are the distinctive, but rather randomly arrayed, city districts of Trollheim:

> - The Bitters
> - Blackraven's Walk
> - Castle Hill
> - Charcoal Valley
> - Elderwood
> - Jelnir's Ferry
> - Old Court
> - Riverside
> - Thistlecourt

## Inhabitants

> - Patrols of [[Blackravens]] are sent out on a regular basis to hunt [[Ice troll|ice trolls]] and other minions of the [[Elvanna|Witch Queen]], and to defend the [[Ulfen]] lands from her malign forces. They are actually based at [[Blackraven Hall]]
> - [[Copper dragon|Copper dragons]], guests of Freyr Darkwine
