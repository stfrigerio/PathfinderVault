> **Fort Thorn** a small settlement with a stone keep beset by feral [[Fey|fey]], [[Owlbear|owlbears]], [[Flame drake|flame drakes]], [[Lizardfolk|lizardfolk]], [[Bugbear|bugbears]], and other threats in the [[Bloodsworn Vale]]. Around [[4707 AR]], then-King of [[Korvosa]] [[Eodred Arabasti II]] appointed [[Gyrad Tolgrith|Sir Gyrad Tolgrith]] to establish Fort Thorn and a trade route through the vale.


## Structures

> Aside from a gatehouse, fortifications, watchtowers, and stables along its walls, Fort Thorn consists of only seven other permanent structures:

> - Open barracks for visitors
> - A two-story stone keep that serves as a garrison for the fort's more than 40 soldiers
> - [[Boar's Bones|The Boars Bones]] inn and tavern
> - [[Wilderness Wares]], a general shop
> - A stone shrine to [[Erastil]]
> - [[Partik's Forge|Partiks Forge]]
> - Four Wheels Hall, a warehouse and hall for caravan drivers
