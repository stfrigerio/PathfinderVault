> **Veka** is an [[Iobaria|Iobarian]] town located on the shores of the [[Nyvyrd]] and has stood as a garrisoned fort since its founding. The easternmost Iobarian settlement in the [[Ice Steppes]], it is home to a number of smithies, where all metal weapons and goods in the region are forged. The owner of these smithies is the ruthless [[Rjul|Koffar Rjul]], who holds control over the entire Nyvyrd region.
