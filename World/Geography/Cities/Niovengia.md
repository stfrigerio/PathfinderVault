> **Niovengia** is the largest of the eight major [[Urdefhan|urdefhan]] walled cities in [[Doga-Delloth]], one of the vaults of [[Orv]]. It is the northernmost city on the vault's [[Sightless Sea]] coast, and stone piers extend out into the sea.
> It is a city of towers and spires carved out of black basalt. There are glowing domes, as well as rookeries for the [[Skaveling|skaveling]] mounts used by the urdefhan. There are also numerous statues of the [[Daemon|daemons]] worshipped by the urdefhans. Niovengia is ruled by Malefactor [[Tryfilion]], whose position is sealed by the *Misery Gage*, an [[Artifact|artifact]] gifted by his patron [[Trelmarixian]].
