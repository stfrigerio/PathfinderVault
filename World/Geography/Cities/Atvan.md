> **Atvan** is a small, peasant town on [[Glacier Lake]] in the [[Irrisen]] province of [[Bleakmarch]]. It is ruled by [[Baelara|Baroness Baelara]], whose husband, [[Gregouri Urvalane]], is a notorious womanizer. The couple spend a great deal of time arguing, which is what got them banished to this remote settlement from [[Whitethrone]].
> The young women of Atvan who attract Gregouri's amorous attentions became targets of the baroness' extravagant rage. To avoid the baroness's wrath, [[Vaclav Konstantin]] proprietor of the [[Sealskinner Inn]], has closed down his brothel and employs only local grandmothers in the inn's common room.
> Atvan main business is to provide shelter, food, and entertainment for merchants sailing to and from Whitethrone. It is also well known that Atvan has coveted its neighbor [[Trezira]]'s ice cleaving monopoly.
