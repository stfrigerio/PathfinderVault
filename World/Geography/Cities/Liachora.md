> **Liachora** was the capital of the city-state of Pol-Liachora in the [[Iblydos|Iblydan archipelago]] that also included the city of [[Aelyosos]]. It was destroyed by the [[Thalassic behemoth|thalassic behemoth]] Ousmariku at the beginning of the [[Age of Lost Omens]]. Its citizens were either killed in the attack or fled afterwards to Aelyosos. The ruins of Liachora have since been claimed by [[Sahuagin|sahuagin]].
