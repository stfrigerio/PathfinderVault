> The **Djurstor Confederacy** was a brief alliance of [[Ulfen]] [[Thane|thanes]] who ruled a territory in the eastern [[Lands of the Linnorm Kings|Linnorm Kingdom]] of [[Raemerrund]], now part of the country of [[Irrisen]]. Their warriors were defeated by [[Baba Yaga]] and her forces in the [[Winter War]] of [[3313 AR]], and their lands were incorporated in her new realm.
