> A **kalavakus** [[Demon|demon]] (also known as a **horned demon** or **slaver demon**) is a muscular, bipedal creature with massive horn-like protuberances erupting from its forehead, lower arms, fingers and toes. They are formed from the [[Soul|souls]] of [[Slaver|slavers]] and often employed in a similar capacity in the [[Abyss]].
