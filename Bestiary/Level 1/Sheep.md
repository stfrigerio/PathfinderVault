> **Sheep** are domesticated ruminant [[Animal|animals]] with a thick wooly coat. The males, known as **rams**, often have large curved horns; while the females, called **ewes**, have none. Juvenile sheep are termed **lambs**. Sheep are kept for their wool, leather, milk, and meat (mutton or lamb), and tend to be kept in large groups called flocks. Sheep can be purchased for around 2 [[Gold piece|gold pieces]].


## Sheep in religion

> Sheep are the [[Sacred animal|sacred animal]] of a few [[Deity|divine entities]], such as the [[Empyreal lord|empyreal lords]] [[Neshen]] and [[Rowdrosh]]. The [[Deity|god]] [[Osiris]] also has the ram as his sacred animal.
