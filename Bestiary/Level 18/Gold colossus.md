> A **gold colossus** is a colossus made from alloys of [[Gold|gold]] and less precious metals. It is capable of taking the form of an immense war monument.


## On Golarion

> A famous gold colossus is the [[Champion of the Gilded Host]], built entirely from gold by the rajah of the [[Vudra|Vudrani]] kingdom Praramdav as a [[Weapon|superweapon]] against the [[Tarrasque]], which never managed to see any combat.
