> **Viduuses** are the [[Psychopomp|psychopomp]] librarians who dwell in the scriptoriums and libraries atop [[Pharasma|Pharasmas spire]]. While the lesser of their brethren deal with recording the deeds of everyday [[Soul|souls]], viduuses focus on taking down and cataloging the lives of extraordinary [[Mortal|mortals]] and information that must not be shared widely.
