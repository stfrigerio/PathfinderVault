> **Ahmuuth** [[Psychopomp|psychopomps]] are the [[Divine servitor|divine servitors]] of [[Pharasma]] who help [[Mortal|mortals]] destroy [[Undead|undead]] or help usher them into the afterlife, in a peaceful way if possible but forcibly if not.


## Appearance

> An ahmuuth resembles a [[Humanoid|humanoid]] clad in sombre dress and an [[Owl|owl]] mask, carrying a [[Dagger|dagger]] and protected by gravestone shards floating around it. Ahmuuths quietly stalk enemies, only revealing themselves when striking. They stand just over six feet tall and weigh approximately 150 pounds.
