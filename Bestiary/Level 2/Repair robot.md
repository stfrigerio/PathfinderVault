*See also: [[Repair drone]]*
> **Repair robots** are quadrupedal, dexterous [[Robot|robots]] designed and programmed to fix all manners of machinery. Single-minded in their task, they constantly seek out and repair everything that they can, even when abandoned by their owners. Sometimes, if there is nothing for them to fix, repair robots leave their home and congregate with others to travel together, repairing each other on their journey.


## Abilities

> Aside from their [[Engineering|engineering]] expertise and robot repair tools, repair robots are also armed with a launcher that fires polymer nets, and a typical repair robot contains five such nets. However, they are vulnerable to direct applications of uncontrolled electricity.
