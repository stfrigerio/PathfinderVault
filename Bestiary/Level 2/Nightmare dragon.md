> **Nightmare dragons** are a species of [[Esoteric dragon|esoteric dragon]] native to the elusive [[Dimension of Dreams]]. There they disturb the dreams of sleeping [[Mortal|mortals]] with nightmares and collude with [[Night hag|night hags]] to collect their [[Soul|souls]].


## Appearance

> These dragons are generally a deep, ebony color with red frills. They trail shadows wherever they go and appear unnaturally contorted.
