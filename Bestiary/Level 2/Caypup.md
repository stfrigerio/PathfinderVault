> **Caypups** are the [[Half-celestial|half-celestial]] offspring of [[Cayhound|cayhounds]] and normal [[PathfinderWiki/Creatures/Dog|dogs]].


## Appearance

> Caypups resemble rust-red mastiff puppies with piercing blue eyes. They measure four feet long, weigh 75 pounds, and never mature past adolescence.


## Habitat

> Caypups can be found in both [[Elysium]] and the [[Material Plane]], and are driven to do [[Good|good]] and fight [[Evil|evil]] wherever they go. They sometimes join [[Adventurer|adventurers]] or become [[Familiar|familiars]] in the hope of emulating their ancestor Thunder and his master [[Cayden Cailean]].
