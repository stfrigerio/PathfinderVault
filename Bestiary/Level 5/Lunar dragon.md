> **Lunar dragons** are social [[Outer dragon|outer dragons]] who watch interesting [[Planet|planets]] and interact with [[Mortal|mortals]].



## Appearance

> Lunar dragons have gossamer wings and swirling cosmic patterns across their scales. Spiny protrusions sweep back from their face.


## Habitat

> Lunar dragons can travel the vacuum of [[Space travel|space]] and lair in [[Moon|moons]].


## Abilities

> Lunar dragons are such alien creatures that others are often confused in their presence. A lunar dragon's body has [[Anathema|anathematic]] properties similar to [[Silver|silver]], its breath is so extremely cold that even creatures immune to the coldest temperatures can still experience harm from it, and it reflects light and other rays of energy.


## On Golarion

> Lunar dragons watch [[Golarion]] from lairs said to be on its [[Moon|moon]], but only occasionally interfere with mortal affairs.
