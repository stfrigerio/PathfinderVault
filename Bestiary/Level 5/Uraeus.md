> The **uraeuses** are members of a legendary race of winged, two-headed cobras said to be birthed by [[Wadjet]]. They are said to have an affinity for water and spit [[Venom|venom]] into the eyes of their foes. The uraeuses are spirits of wisdom guarding, protecting, and advising the [[Pharaoh|pharaohs]] and priesthood of [[Osirion]].
