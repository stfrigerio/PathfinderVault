> The **Stabbing Beast** is the [[Herald|herald]] is the [[Deity|god]] [[Norgorber]], and is an exceedingly dangerous predator who takes the shape of either a [[Humanoid|humanoid]] with a scorpion-like tail or of a gigantic, silver [[Scorpion|scorpion]]. Despite its appearance, it possesses a soft and captivating voice. The Stabbing Beast is sent to [[Golarion]] to aid Norgorber's worshippers in hiding secrets or performing audacious thefts.
> The Stabbing Beast has been killed on a number of occasions. Norgorber has always resurrected it after its deaths, leaving its memories intact to drive it to avoid the mistakes that led to its previous incarnations' demises.
