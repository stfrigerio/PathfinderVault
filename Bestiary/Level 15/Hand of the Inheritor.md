> The **Hand of the Inheritor** appeared as a massive golden [[Angel|angel]], constantly surrounded by a halo of spinning [[Sword|blades]]. He was the [[Herald|herald]] of the [[God|warrior-goddess]] [[Iomedae]] until he was captured and tortured by the [[Demon lord|demon lord]] [[Baphomet]] during the [[Fifth Mendevian Crusade]]. After the end of the war, Iomedae replaced him with the [[Mortal|mortal]] [[Galfrey|Crusader Queen Galfrey]] of [[Mendev]].


## History

> Before [[Aroden|Arodens]] disappearance, he was known as the **Hand of Vengeance** and a servant of the [[Empyreal Lord]] of vengeance [[Ragathiel]], but became a valorous and civil servant of Iomedae, whom he referred to as "the Just Queen of Heaven".
> He referred to his halo of swords as an independent creature named **"Brightsword"**, and used it as a spiked [[Shield|shield]] or could summon from it a [[Longsword|longsword]].
