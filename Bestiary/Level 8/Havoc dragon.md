> **Havoc dragons** are the species of [[Planar dragon|planar dragons]] native to [[Elysium]].


## Appearance

> Havoc dragons possess colourful scales and insectile wings. Their whiplike tail waves as if stirred by a breeze.


## Ecology

> Despite their best intentions, havoc dragons often cause collateral damage as they develop whimsical wonderlands of revelry and relaxation.
